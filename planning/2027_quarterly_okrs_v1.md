# 2027 Quarterly Themes & OKRs (v1)

## Overview

- **Theme:** Lock the 2027 plan: bets, metrics, and budget—provably.
- **Big Bets (pillars):** Trust as a channel, Enterprise control plane moat, Ecosystem ARR, Autonomous ops default, Unit economics as product.
- **Decision Receipts:** Required for KPI tree approval, quarterly OKRs, budget, risk register, and stage gate updates.

## Q1 2027 — "Revenue Engine"

**Objective 1: Convert self-serve funnel to revenue with provable enforcement.**

- KR1: Self-serve activation ≥ 55% within 14 days via guided onboarding and pricing enforcement stage gate.
- KR2: Pricing/entitlement enforcement enabled on 100% of plans with receipts and audit trails.
- KR3: Net churn ≤ 1.5% monthly; expansion bookings ≥ 12% of ARR.

**Objective 2: Defend churn with trust and telemetry.**

- KR1: Churn-risk detection coverage ≥ 95% of active tenants with automated alerts.
- KR2: 80% of renewal cycles include trust center snapshot + receipts bundle.
- KR3: MTTR for revenue-impacting incidents < 45 minutes with auto-rollbacks.

## Q2 2027 — "Enterprise Scale"

**Objective 1: Multi-region and sovereign readiness.**

- KR1: Multi-region SLOs (p99 latency < 250ms, 99.9% availability) validated in 3 regions.
- KR2: Residency controls (BYOK, private networking, data borders) available in 80% of enterprise deals.
- KR3: DR readiness proven quarterly with ≤15-minute RPO and ≤1-hour RTO, evidenced via receipts.

**Objective 2: Compliance depth and trust posture.**

- KR1: Vulnerability SLA adherence ≥ 98%; SBOM freshness < 30 days.
- KR2: Automated attestation and trust center snapshots embedded in procurement flows for 90% of enterprise opportunities.
- KR3: Third-party control inheritance mapped to policy engine with <2% false positives.

## Q3 2027 — "Platform Ecosystem"

**Objective 1: Marketplace storefront and publisher quality.**

- KR1: Marketplace storefront GA with verified publisher flow; 50 certified publishers live.
- KR2: Adapter attach rate ≥ 2.4 adapters per active tenant; marketplace GMV run rate ≥ 8% of ARR.
- KR3: Revenue share automation with invoice pack receipts for 100% of eligible transactions.

**Objective 2: Ecosystem trust and safety.**

- KR1: Adapter security scans and provenance receipts for 100% of marketplace submissions.
- KR2: SLA-backed rollback + kill-switch coverage for all marketplace adapters.
- KR3: Quality score (latency, error rate, CSAT) tracked and surfaced in marketplace rankings.

## Q4 2027 — "Autonomous Company"

**Objective 1: Autonomous ops default.**

- KR1: Autonomous remediation coverage ≥ 75% of top 20 incident classes with ≤10% human handoff rate.
- KR2: Error budget burn forecasting and guardrails prevent >2% monthly budget breaches.
- KR3: Support cost per ticket reduced by 35% via autonomy and L2 copilot.

**Objective 2: Continuous controls & unit economics as product.**

- KR1: FinOps transparency: per-tenant cost views with <2% variance between metered and invoiced units.
- KR2: Automated cost anomaly detection (p95 alert latency < 5 minutes) across regions.
- KR3: Enterprise scorecards (trust + cost + reliability) shipped monthly with Decision Receipts.

## Stage Gate Mapping

- **Spec**: OKR definitions + KPI contracts approved with receipts.
- **Build**: Instrumentation, policy enforcement, and marketplace workflows implemented with test coverage.
- **Prove**: Gate checklists satisfied (revenue, trust, enterprise) and validated in staging.
- **Operate**: Reliability SLOs, DR drills, and continuous monitoring active; receipts emitted.
- **Package**: Documentation, trust center snapshot, and cost transparency bundles published.

## Dependencies & Enablers

- Pricing enforcement + invoice pack from Revenue Readiness gate.
- Trust center automation for procurement velocity.
- Marketplace ledger and revenue share engine.
- FinOps observability (cost guardrails, anomaly detection).
- Autonomous incident response with playbooks linked to policy engine.
