# Regulatory Adaptation Framework (RAF) Charter

## Mission

The RAF team is responsible for monitoring global regulatory changes and translating them into machine-readable policies (OPA Rego) within 48 hours of enactment.

## Responsibilities

1.  **Horizon Scanning:** Monitor EU, US, and Global regulatory bodies for upcoming AI and data privacy legislation.
2.  **Policy Translation:** Convert legal text into technical specifications and OPA policies.
3.  **Impact Analysis:** Run "Policy Simulations" to see how new rules would affect current system traffic.
4.  **Advisory:** Advise product teams on upcoming constraints before they become law.

## Success Metrics

- **Time-to-Policy:** Time from law enactment to OPA policy deployment (Target: < 48 hours).
- **Translation Accuracy:** % of policies that pass external legal review.
- **Block Rate:** % of PRs blocked by new compliance rules (Indicator of proactive vs. reactive adaptation).
