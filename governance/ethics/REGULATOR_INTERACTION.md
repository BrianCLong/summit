# Regulator Interaction Protocols

## Scope

This document defines the strict protocols for any interaction with government regulators or oversight bodies.

## Interaction Channels

- **Public Channels Only:** All substantive discussions regarding policy or product compliance should occur in public forums or formal, recorded settings.
- **No Private Backchannels:** Informal text messages, private dinners, or off-the-record briefings with regulators are strictly prohibited.

## Documentation Requirements

1.  **Pre-Meeting:** An agenda must be filed with Legal and Governance 48 hours in advance.
2.  **During Meeting:** Two company representatives must be present.
3.  **Post-Meeting:** A summary of the interaction must be logged in the `provenance-ledger` within 24 hours.

## Escalation

Any request by a regulator for "off the books" favors, data access, or private rule-making must be immediately reported to the General Counsel and the Ethics Committee.
