# Clean Hands Charter

**Principle:** We do not compete on influence. We compete on adaptability.

## Core Commitments
1.  **Transparency:** All regulator interactions are logged and auditable.
2.  **Compliance:** We adhere to the strictest interpretation of the law in all jurisdictions.
3.  **Ethics:** We refuse to engage in capture, bribery, or unethical lobbying.
4.  **Evidence:** We provide cryptographic proof of compliance by default.

## Enforcement
This charter is enforced by CI/CD pipelines. Violations block deployment.
