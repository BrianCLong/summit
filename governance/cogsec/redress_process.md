# Cognitive Security Redress Process

**Purpose**: Provide a mechanism for individuals to challenge decisions made by the Cognitive Security system (e.g., if their content was incorrectly flagged).

## 1. Scope
Applies to any automated or manual action that results in:
*   Content takedown request.
*   Internal blocking of a domain.
*   labeling of content as "disinformation".

## 2. Submission
*   **Channel**: Email to `privacy@summit.internal` or via Help Desk ticket "Cognitive Security Appeal".
*   **Required Info**: URL, justification, evidence of error.

## 3. Review Process
1.  **Triage**: Privacy Team reviews within 48 hours.
2.  **Investigation**: Re-verification of the artifact by a different analyst.
3.  **Decision**:
    *   **Upheld**: Original decision stands.
    *   **Overturned**: Action reversed (takedown rescinded, block removed).
4.  **Notification**: Outcome communicated to requester.

## 4. Logging
All appeals and outcomes must be logged in the `Governance Evidence Pack` (`EVD-COGSEC-GOV-001`).
