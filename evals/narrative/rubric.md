# Narrative Coherence Evaluation Rubric

This rubric outlines the scoring criteria for the narrative coherence evaluation harness. Each dimension is scored on a scale from 0.0 to 1.0, with 1.0 being perfectly coherent and 0.0 being completely incoherent.

## 1. Logical Flow (0.0 - 1.0)
Evaluates the smooth transition of ideas between sentences.
*   **1.0**: Ideas connect naturally and logically; clear transitions are present.
*   **0.5**: Some abrupt shifts in topic, but the overall point is understandable.
*   **0.0**: Sentences are disjointed and seem completely unrelated to each other.

## 2. Entity Consistency (0.0 - 1.0)
Evaluates how consistently entities (people, places, things) are referred to throughout the response.
*   **1.0**: Pronouns and names are used clearly and unambiguously; no confusion about who/what is being discussed.
*   **0.5**: Minor ambiguity in pronoun resolution or slight naming inconsistencies that don't severely impact understanding.
*   **0.0**: Frequent, confusing changes in entity references; pronoun "soup" making it impossible to track subjects.

## 3. Temporal Ordering (0.0 - 1.0)
Evaluates the chronological sense of events described.
*   **1.0**: Events are presented in a logical chronological order or explicitly marked with clear temporal indicators (e.g., "earlier", "subsequently").
*   **0.5**: Sequence of events is slightly jumbled but can be reconstructed by the reader with effort.
*   **0.0**: Events jump randomly back and forth in time without markers, creating a confusing timeline.

## 4. Absence of Contradictions (0.0 - 1.0)
Evaluates whether the response makes conflicting claims.
*   **1.0**: No contradictory statements within the response.
*   **0.5**: Minor logical inconsistencies or slightly conflicting details that don't ruin the main point.
*   **0.0**: Direct, major contradictions that make the entire response unreliable (e.g., "They survived" followed by "They all died").

## 5. Readability (0.0 - 1.0)
Evaluates the overall clarity and grammatical structure of the text.
*   **1.0**: Clear, well-structured sentences, appropriate vocabulary, easy to read.
*   **0.5**: Contains awkward phrasing, run-on sentences, or minor grammatical errors that slow reading.
*   **0.0**: Incomprehensible due to severe grammatical errors or bizarre phrasing.

## Overall Coherence Score
The overall score is an average of the five dimensions above.
