# AGIR Benchmark: Audit-Grade Investigation Reproducibility

## Objective
To quantify the determinism, policy compliance, and auditability of Summit's OSINT & Investigation subsumption playbooks against established benchmarks and competitor capabilities.

## Metrics
1. **Replay Hash Stability (RHS):** The percentage of playbook runs that produce identical cryptographic hashes for `report.json` and `metrics.json` when rerun with the exact same inputs and fixtures. Target: 100%.
2. **Provenance Completeness (PC):** The percentage of graph edges and nodes generated by the playbook that contain a complete provenance envelope (source_system, collection_method, timestamp, evidence_id). Target: 100%.
3. **Policy Compliance (PoC):** The percentage of tool calls that pass all pre-execution policy checks (ToS, rate limits, allowlists). Target: 100%.
4. **Time-to-Defensible-Report (TDR):** The elapsed time from playbook initiation to the generation of a complete, cryptographically verifiable `report.json` containing citations to immutable evidence objects. Target: < 5 minutes for standard triage.

## Fixture Datasets
* **`fixture_sociallinks_like.json`:** Synthetic dataset mimicking Social Links Crimewall outputs (graph, table, map views).
* **`fixture_spiderfoot.json`:** Recorded outputs from open SpiderFoot modules (e.g., DNS, WHOIS) on public domains (e.g., nixintel.info).
* **`fixture_orpheus_insurer.json`:** Synthetic dataset modeling an Orpheus Cyber portfolio monitoring report and risk scores.
* **`fixture_oceanir_claims.json`:** Synthetic dataset of image geolocation claims with uncertainty bounds.

## Regression Tests
* **SpiderFoot Connector:** Validate determinism when parsing `fixture_spiderfoot.json` into the Summit graph.
* **Social Links Connector:** Verify correct handling of ToS constraints and rate-limit simulation using mocked API responses.
* **Orpheus Connector:** Ensure risk score propagation and evidence linking from `fixture_orpheus_insurer.json`.
* **Oceanir Connector:** Validate uncertainty bounds and metadata extraction from `fixture_oceanir_claims.json`.

## CI Integration
* The AGIR benchmark suite runs nightly.
* A failure in Replay Hash Stability (RHS) or Provenance Completeness (PC) will break the build.
* `stamp.json` output is validated against the `stamp.schema.json` to ensure versioning and tool tracking.
