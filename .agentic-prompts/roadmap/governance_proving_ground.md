## Mission

Execute the governance proving ground sprint to validate enforcement, auditability, and compliance
workflows using policy-as-code and authoritative governance sources.

## Phases

1. **Scenario design**
   - Define proving ground scenarios and expected governance outcomes.
   - Map each scenario to policy-as-code controls.
2. **Execution and evidence**
   - Run scenarios with full evidence capture and decision logging.
   - Record deviations as governed exceptions.
3. **Outcome synthesis**
   - Summarize results with remediation actions and governance impact notes.

## Deliverables

- Governance proving ground scenario catalog.
- Evidence logs and exception register.
- Outcome report with remediation actions.

## Guardrails

- Governance sources remain authoritative.
- Evidence capture is mandatory for every scenario.
