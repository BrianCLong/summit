## Mission

Deliver a governed onboarding experience for reviewers that aligns expectations, evidence intake,
and escalation pathways with the readiness authority files.

## Phases

1. **Authority alignment**
   - Confirm the exact authority files reviewers must use.
   - Map reviewer responsibilities to explicit governance mandates.
2. **Onboarding flow**
   - Define the step-by-step onboarding journey for new reviewers.
   - Standardize evidence intake, review cadence, and escalation protocol.
3. **Verification readiness**
   - Produce validation steps that confirm reviewer readiness before live assessments.

## Deliverables

- Reviewer onboarding checklist and briefing pack.
- Evidence intake template and escalation matrix.
- Verification checklist for reviewer readiness.

## Guardrails

- Maintain strict alignment to authority files and readiness assertion.
- Capture any deviations as governed exceptions.
