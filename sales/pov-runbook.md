# PoV Runbook

## CRM Data Hygiene Pilots: Proof of Value Framework

**Goal**: Turn HubSpot CRM hygiene pilots into measurable, marketable proof for Summit. Frame Summit as the **AI execution layer** that sits on top of HubSpot’s own AI features, not a competing CRM.

### 1. Baseline → After Metrics Pack
Compute RevOps-standard hygiene metrics:
*   **Before (Sandbox Baseline):**
    *   Field completion rate
    *   Duplicate rate
    *   Stale record ratio
    *   Time spent on manual cleanup
*   **After (4-8 Weeks Post-Pilot):**
    *   Track deltas (e.g., field completion from 60%→85%, duplicate rate down to <3%, stale records down to <20%).

### 2. Revenue-Adjacent Outcomes
Correlate hygiene improvements with downstream outcomes RevOps cares about:
*   Improved forecast accuracy
*   Cleaner pipeline views
*   More reliable automation
*   Reduced leakage and missed routing
*   **Capture at least one concrete story per pilot** (e.g., "fixed geo-tagging and routing, surfaced a $500k opportunity that had been invisible in dashboards").

### 3. Customer Narrative Capture
For 2–3 ideal pilot teams, run short structured interviews and extract quotes on:
*   Pain before ("data decay, manual cleanup, broken automations")
*   Studio + hygiene experience
*   Concrete benefits ("hours saved," "trust in reports," "RevOps finally owns hygiene end‑to‑end")
*   **Output:** Light case-study outlines and anonymized internal briefs for sales/positioning.

### 4. Align With 2026 RevOps + HubSpot Trends
*   Map Summit’s hygiene metrics and stories to RevOps trends: AI agents closing "the last 10% gap," HubSpot AI expansion, and RevOps‑led data hygiene as a growth lever.
*   **Positioning:** Summit is the **AI execution layer** that sits on top of HubSpot’s own AI features.

### 5. Pilot Readout & Next-Step Signals
Generate a simple "RevOps hygiene scorecard" deck for each pilot, including:
*   Before/after metrics
*   Screenshots of sandbox diffs
*   Qualitative quotes
*   Next recommended automations

Define clear thresholds indicating readiness to:
1.  Enable limited safe writes in production.
2.  Expand from hygiene into forecasting agents.
3.  Scale to additional similar RevOps teams.

### 6. Success Criteria
*   At least 3 pilot accounts with quantified hygiene improvements and strong qualitative endorsements suitable for external or semi‑external use.
*   A reusable metric framework (baseline + after) that can be applied to all future RevOps customers to prove Summit’s value in under one quarter.
