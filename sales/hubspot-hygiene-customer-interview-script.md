# Customer Narrative Capture: Structured Interview Guide

**Objective:** For 2–3 ideal pilot teams, run short structured interviews and extract quotes on pain points, the Summit experience, and concrete benefits. Turn these into light case-study outlines and anonymized internal briefs for sales and positioning.

## Interview Questions & Prompts

### 1. Pain Before (The Baseline)
**Goal: Capture the cost of inaction and the reality of the "messy middle."**
*   "Before using Summit, how would you describe the state of your HubSpot data hygiene?"
*   "Can you describe the pain points caused by data decay, manual cleanup, or broken automations?"
*   "How much time were you or your team spending each week trying to manage data quality manually?"
*   *Listen for keywords:* "data decay," "manual cleanup," "broken automations," "lack of trust."

### 2. Studio + Hygiene Experience (The Solution)
**Goal: Understand the perception of Summit's sandbox and workflow.**
*   "What was your experience like using the Summit Studio and running the hygiene pilots in the sandbox?"
*   "How did the ability to preview changes (diffs) before applying them to production affect your confidence in the tool?"
*   "Did you feel like Summit acted as a complementary AI execution layer on top of HubSpot, rather than a competing system?"
*   *Listen for keywords:* "confidence," "safe," "execution layer," "sandbox diffs."

### 3. Concrete Benefits (The Outcome)
**Goal: Quantify the value and capture revenue-adjacent impact.**
*   "Since running the pilot, what concrete benefits have you observed?"
*   "Can you estimate the hours saved per week on manual cleanup tasks?"
*   "Has this improved trust in your reports and forecasting? How so?"
*   "Do you feel like RevOps finally owns data hygiene end-to-end now? Why?"
*   "Can you share a specific story where cleaner data directly impacted a deal or pipeline visibility (e.g., finding a lost opportunity, fixing a critical routing error)?"
*   *Listen for keywords:* "hours saved," "trust in reports," "RevOps owns hygiene," "revenue impact."

## Output Requirements

After the interview, synthesize the notes into:

1.  **Light Case-Study Outline:** A 1-page summary including Before/After metrics, a core narrative (the "Concrete Story"), and 2-3 strong quotes.
2.  **Anonymized Internal Brief:** A concise summary for the sales team to use in positioning Summit to similar RevOps leaders, highlighting the "AI execution layer" narrative and the "last 10% gap."

## Alignment with 2026 Trends

Ensure the interview capture aligns with these key themes:
*   AI agents closing "the last 10% gap" in data completion.
*   The expansion of HubSpot AI and how Summit augments it.
*   RevOps-led data hygiene as a primary growth lever.
