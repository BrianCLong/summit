### 7. Hard Questions We Will Be Asked & Suggested Answers

**Q: Does your system create or distribute content to test these narratives?**
**A:** No. Summit is a strictly observational and analytical platform. Our Ethical Framework and explicit Non-Manipulation Policy prohibit the creation or distribution of content. We only analyze existing data in the information environment to model propagation and behavioral patterns.

**Q: How do you differentiate between organic market sentiment and 'information warfare'?**
**A:** Our Automated Narrative Tracking engine uses Behavioral Pattern Recognition to identify anomalies in propagation speed, source clustering, and network dynamics. While organic sentiment tends to follow specific diffusion curves, coordinated campaigns often exhibit distinct, identifiable signatures that our system flags for analyst review.

**Q: If you can predict narrative-driven repricing, aren't you just a trading signal generator?**
**A:** We are a *risk* anticipation platform. Our Probabilistic Volatility & Liquidity Signal Engine forecasts the *likelihood of instability* based on narrative velocity. We provide early-warning intelligence for risk management and defensive posturing, not automated trading execution.

**Q: What is your data provenance, and how do you ensure compliance with data privacy regulations?**
**A:** We ingest publicly available information and structure it using our proprietary models. Our Compliance-First Architecture includes strict data governance and comprehensive audit logging, ensuring all analysis is traceable and adheres to relevant privacy regulations.

**Q: Why would a traditional hedge fund buy this over their existing alternative data feeds?**
**A:** Alternative data feeds typically provide raw sentiment scores or specific operational metrics. Summit provides *Information Environment Modeling*. We don't just tell you the sentiment; we model the crowd psychology and the *velocity* of the narrative, identifying structural market instability that raw data feeds miss.
