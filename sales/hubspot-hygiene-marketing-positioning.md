# HubSpot CRM Hygiene: Summit Positioning & Internal Narrative

**Objective:** Align Summit’s hygiene metrics and stories to the language and trends RevOps leaders are seeing in 2026. Ensure internal docs and decks explicitly frame Summit as the AI execution layer that sits on top of HubSpot’s own AI features, not a competing CRM.

## Core Narrative: The AI Execution Layer

While HubSpot CRM is expanding its own AI capabilities to manage data globally, RevOps teams are finding a "last 10% gap" where generic AI falls short of their specific business logic and workflows.

**The Problem:**

* HubSpot's native AI is excellent for general tasks, but struggles with the unique nuances of your specific deduplication rules, custom firmographics, and complex routing requirements.
* The "messy middle" of CRM data decay (34% per year) requires constant manual intervention, eroding the trust in forecasting and pipeline reporting.
* RevOps teams need to confidently own data hygiene end-to-end without spending hours manually reviewing duplicates.

**The Summit Solution:**

* Summit is the **AI execution layer** that sits precisely on top of HubSpot’s AI features. It doesn't compete with your CRM; it makes your CRM data actionable.
* We close the "last 10% gap" by allowing RevOps to define deterministic hygiene policies, run them safely in a sandboxed environment, review exact visual diffs, and only apply changes to production when confident.
* We turn data hygiene from an administrative chore into a strategic growth lever.

## Key 2026 RevOps Trends

When speaking to prospects, align our stories with these key trends:

1. **AI Agents Closing the "Last 10% Gap":** Emphasize that generalized AI isn't enough for clean CRM data. RevOps needs agentic engineering tailored to their specific rules engine. Summit provides this deterministic safety net.
2. **HubSpot AI Expansion:** Acknowledge that HubSpot is adding AI, but position Summit as the specialized execution engine that translates those general capabilities into reliable, auditable data operations.
3. **RevOps-Led Data Hygiene as a Growth Lever:** Frame hygiene not as "cleanup," but as unlocking revenue. Cleaner data = better routing = faster response times = higher win rates.

## Success Stories & Proof Points

* **Metrics-Driven Proof:** We rely on hard metrics (Field Completion Rate, Duplicate Rate <3%, Stale Records <20%) measured from Baseline (Sandbox) to After (Production).
* **Revenue-Adjacent Outcomes:** We don't just talk about clean rows; we talk about improved forecast accuracy, cleaner pipeline views, and the $500k opportunities surfaced by fixing geo-tagging and routing.
* **Customer Quotes:** Use the structured interview quotes internally to validate the pain ("data decay, manual cleanup") and the relief ("hours saved, trust restored, RevOps finally owns hygiene").
