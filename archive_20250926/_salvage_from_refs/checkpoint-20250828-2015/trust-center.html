Key Security & Privacy Controls

Streaming Hardening (Kafka)

Our event streaming pipeline is hardened to ensure data integrity and reliability.

  • Exactly-Once Semantics (EOS): Enabled end-to-end to prevent data loss or duplication.
  • Schema Registry: All events are validated against a central schema registry.
  • Dead-Letter Queues (DLQ): Failed messages are isolated for analysis without halting the pipeline.

Data Residency & Governance

We enforce strict data residency and governance policies to protect data sovereignty.

  • OPA Policy Enforcement: Cross-region data processing is blocked by policy.
  • Regional Scoping: EU and US data are routed to region-specific topics.

Privacy Enhancing Technologies (PETs)

PII is protected at the point of ingestion using industry-standard techniques.

  • PII Tokenization: Personally Identifiable Information is detected and tokenized at the edge.
  • Privacy Budgets (Îµ): Foundational audit hooks are in place to track privacy budget consumption.
