# Commercial Brief: Auto-Scientist SDK

## 1. Product Concept

**Name**: Auto-Scientist SDK
**Tagline**: "The Safety Layer for Autonomous Research."
**Description**: A developer toolkit that wraps LLMs with a "Scientific Constitution" and an execution sandbox, enabling enterprises to build safe R&D agents.

## 2. Target Audience

**Primary: Pharma R&D Teams**

1.  **Pfizer** - Drug Discovery Division
2.  **Novartis** - AI Innovation Lab
3.  **Merck** - Computational Biology
4.  **Roche** - pRED (Pharma Research and Early Development)
5.  **AstraZeneca** - Data Science & AI

**Secondary: Material Science & Chemicals** 6. **Dow Chemical** - Digital Research 7. **BASF** - Digitalization in R&D 8. **DuPont** - Innovation Center

**Tertiary: Academic & Non-Profit** 9. **Broad Institute** - Genomics Platform 10. **Allen Institute for AI** - Semantic Scholar / Open Science

## 3. Value Proposition

- **Safety**: Prevents AI from hallucinating dangerous experiments.
- **Automation**: Reduces "human-in-the-loop" time by 90% via trusted oversight.
- **Compliance**: Auto-generated audit trails for every experiment.

## 4. Pricing Hypothesis

- **Tier 1 (Community)**: Open source core (MIT).
- **Tier 2 (Enterprise)**: Managed Oversight Service + Indemnification ($5k/month/seat).

## 5. Packaging Plan

- `pip install auto-scientist`
- Docker container with pre-configured Airflow + Sandbox.
