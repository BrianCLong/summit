# Frontier Alignment SDK - Commercial Brief

## Product Overview
The **Frontier Alignment SDK** is an enterprise-grade toolkit for fine-tuning and overseeing Large Language Models (LLMs) with a focus on safety, tool usage, and continuous improvement via telemetry.

## Key Value Propositions
1.  **Safety-First Alignment**: Built-in oversight layer that catches bad outputs before they reach users.
2.  **Telemetry-Aware Loop**: Automatically improves models on their weak spots using production data.
3.  **Tool & Graph Native**: Designed for agents that use tools and manipulate knowledge graphs, not just chat.

## Target Audience
- **Enterprises**: Banks, Healthcare, Gov who need strict compliance and audit trails for LLM outputs.
- **Platform Builders**: Companies building "Agentic" workflows who need reliable tool use.
