# Summit GTM Account Plan (Top 4 Targets)

## Scope

This plan operationalizes the top-priority targets into immediate execution for the next 30 days:

1. ServiceNow
2. Panther Labs
3. Ivalua
4. GEP

The objective is to convert live buying signals into meetings, technical discovery, and one pilot-design conversation per target.

## Shared Positioning Thesis

Summit should be positioned as the open, sovereign execution layer for agentic systems where governance is non-negotiable:

- Knowledge graph as the durable context substrate
- Policy-as-code as the runtime control plane
- Evidence bundles as the auditability and trust artifact

## Target 1: ServiceNow

### Reason-now signal

ServiceNow is publicly advancing AI specialists embedded inside governed workflows.

### ICP fit hypothesis

Strong fit for customers needing policy-constrained AI actions, especially in regulated enterprise operations.

### Primary buyer / champion

- Buyer: CPO / SVP Product for AI & Automation
- Champion: Director of AI Platform Governance / Principal Architect for AI Workflows

### First-touch message

"You are proving that probabilistic AI performs best inside governed workflows. Summit adds an open-source policy-as-code and knowledge-graph substrate with evidence bundles so enterprise customers can independently verify what the AI specialist did, why it acted, and under which policy constraints."

### 30-day sequence

- **Day 1-3:** Engage in ServiceNow ecosystem channels (community + LinkedIn signal threads).
- **Day 4-7:** Send targeted outreach with a one-page architecture overlay mapped to Now Platform workflow controls.
- **Day 8-14:** Run a 30-minute technical discovery on governance gaps (policy portability, evidence interoperability).
- **Day 15-21:** Deliver a short design brief: "Governed Exception" reference workflow with replayable evidence.
- **Day 22-30:** Propose pilot scope (single workflow family, 2-3 policies, exportable evidence pack).

### Exit criteria

- Intro call booked with platform/product architect
- Agreement to review a scoped pilot design
- Named evaluator from governance/compliance side
