# Product Overview

Summit offers three tiers of service to meet the needs of different organizations.

## Tiers

### Core (Starter)
**Best for: Individuals and small teams exploring the platform.**
- Basic rate limits (100 req/min)
- 5 seats
- 50 GB storage
- Standard support

### Pro
**Best for: Growing teams building production applications.**
- Higher rate limits (1,000 req/min)
- 20 seats
- 250 GB storage
- SSO/SAML Integration
- Advanced Graph Analytics
- Priority Support

### Enterprise
**Best for: Large organizations requiring control, compliance, and scale.**
- Custom high rate limits (10k+ req/min)
- Unlimited seats
- 1 TB+ storage
- Full SSO/SAML & RBAC
- Comprehensive Audit Logs (1 year+)
- Data Residency Options
- SLA Guarantees

## Upgrades & Downgrades
You can upgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.
Downgrades are effective at the end of the billing cycle.

## Trial
All new accounts start with a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan.
