# Summit Execution Blueprint (Jules-Prime)

## Strategic Inflection Point

We have **[X days]** until Summit. Assume Summit is a strategic inflection point — not an event.

1. **The 30-Day Measurable Shift:**
   If Summit were wildly successful, what *measurable shift* would exist 30 days after?
   - Revenue shift?
   - Partnership shift?
   - Narrative shift?
   - Category positioning shift?

2. **Irreversible Advantages:**
   What 3 irreversible advantages must we lock in during Summit itself?
   *(Think: Moat, Coalition, Distribution, Standard, Proof, Signal.)*

3. **The Wasted Opportunity:**
   What would make Summit a *wasted opportunity* even if attendance looks good?

4. **Areas of Under-Preparation:**
   Where are we currently under-prepared in:
   - Story
   - Proof
   - Offer
   - Follow-up system
   - Conversion mechanics

5. **Immediate Build Requirements:**
   What must be built NOW (not later) to ensure:
   - Every conversation converts into a next step
   - Every signal is captured
   - Every ally is categorized
   - Every skeptic is understood

6. **The Bold Move:**
   What is the one bold move that competitors would NOT expect us to execute at Summit?

---

## Tactical Add-On (Execution Layer)

**Force these deliverables before Summit:**
- [ ] 1-page narrative positioning sheet
- [ ] Pre-drafted follow-up emails (3 variants: investor / partner / client)
- [ ] Offer clarity (clear CTA with deadline)
- [ ] Data capture system (QR, form, tagging logic)
- [ ] Public proof asset (case study, announcement, partnership tease)
- [ ] Social amplification calendar (before, during, after)
- [ ] 30/60/90 day post-summit conversion plan

**Constraint:**
Assume we are 30% under-resourced. What is the highest-leverage move that would still make Summit decisive?
