# Pricing/Packaging Hypothesis

## Tier 1: Starter (Core Intelligence)
*   **Features**: Basic modules (Vuln, Brand), Standard Integrations (Email, Slack, Jira), Daily Scheduled Scans.
*   **Target**: Small to mid-sized teams needing automated OSINT.
*   **Pricing**: Based on active users or a fixed monthly fee.

## Tier 2: Professional (Actionable Intelligence)
*   **Features**: All Core modules, Advanced Integrations (SIEM/SOAR), Continuous Monitoring (Diffs), Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).
*   **Target**: Enterprise SOCs and dedicated CTI teams.
*   **Pricing**: Based on data ingested or number of monitored entities.

## Tier 3: Enterprise (Provable Intelligence)
*   **Features**: Cryptographic Evidence Signing, Custom Sandboxed Rules, Air-Gapped Deployment Support, Dedicated Instance/Tenant Isolation.
*   **Target**: Regulated industries (Finance, Gov), Legal Teams, Large Enterprises.
*   **Pricing**: Custom enterprise pricing based on deployment scale, modules enabled, and SLAs.
