# ICP Selection Framework & Interview Guide

## Target Personas
1.  **Enterprise CTI/SOC**: Focus on augmenting existing SIEMs and SOARs with high-fidelity, actionable alerts.
2.  **Regulated Investigations/Legal**: Focus on producing undeniable, audit-grade proof for actions like takedowns.
3.  **Brand Protection**: Focus on rapid identification and takedown of typosquats and impersonation sites.

## Interview Guide Questions
1.  What are the primary bottlenecks in your current intelligence gathering and response workflows?
2.  How important is the ability to cryptographically verify the origin and chain of custody of an intelligence artifact?
3.  What are the mandatory integration points (SIEM, SOAR, Ticketing) for a new tool to be adopted in your environment?
4.  Do you have strict data residency or air-gapped deployment requirements?
