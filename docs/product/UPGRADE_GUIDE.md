# Upgrade & Downgrade Guide

## Upgrading
1. Navigate to **Admin Settings > Billing**.
2. Select the new plan (e.g., Upgrade to Pro).
3. Confirm payment details.
4. **Effect**: Capabilities are unlocked immediately.

## Downgrading
1. Navigate to **Admin Settings > Billing**.
2. Select a lower plan (e.g., Downgrade to Core).
3. **Effect**:
   - **Immediate Change**: Downgrades currently take effect immediately. Please ensure your usage is within the new limits before downgrading to avoid service interruption.
   - **Data**: Data exceeding new storage limits may be read-only or archived.
   - **Users**: If you have more users than the new limit, you must deactivate users before the downgrade takes effect.

## Trial Expiration
- If your trial expires and you have not upgraded, you will be automatically moved to the **Core** (Free) tier.
- Enterprise features (SSO, Audit Logs) will be disabled.
- Usage limits will be reduced.
