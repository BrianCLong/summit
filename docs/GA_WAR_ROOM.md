# GA War Room Protocol

## Cadence

- **T-Minus 8 Weeks**: Weekly "Readiness Review" (Wednesday 10am PT).
  - Agenda: Review GA Criteria (Tier-0, SLOs), Sev-1/2 Bug Burn, Blocker Triage.
- **T-Minus 2 Weeks**: Daily Standup (9:30am PT).
  - Agenda: Critical Path blockers only.
- **Launch Week**: Daily "War Room" (Open Zoom/Huddle 9am-5pm PT).
  - Role: Incident Commander on active duty.

## Roles

- **Launch Commander**: Final decision maker on Go/No-Go.
- **Ops Lead**: Manages deployment, rollback, and status page.
- **Support Lead**: Aggregates customer feedback and noise.
- **Comms Lead**: internal/external announcements.

- **Risk Register**: Track known risks and mitigations.
