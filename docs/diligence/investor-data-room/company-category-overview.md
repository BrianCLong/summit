## Product Narrative (Post-GA, Guarded)

Summit is an intelligence analysis platform with AI-augmented graph analytics. The narrative is grounded in current GA scope and documented readiness criteria. Claims are limited to what is evidenced in GA artifacts and operational documentation.

## Category Definition & Exclusions

**Category definition**: Intelligence analysis platform centered on graph analytics, with AI-assisted workflows for investigation, entity/relationship management, and results generation.

**Explicit exclusions (what Summit is not)**

- Not a standalone data broker or data marketplace.
- Not a generic BI dashboarding tool.
- Not a consumer-facing social network analysis product.
- Not an autonomous decision engine; human oversight is mandatory.
