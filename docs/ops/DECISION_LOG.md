# Decision Log

**Owner:** `release-ops-team`
**Escalation:** `vp-of-engineering`

---

This is an append-only log of all escalated decisions made according to the [Escalation Protocol](./ESCALATION_PROTOCOL.md). The purpose of this log is to provide a transparent and auditable record of key decisions.

---

### Template for New Entries

```markdown
---
**Decision Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Decision Maker:** <Name of Decision Maker>
**Involved Teams:** <List of Involved Teams>
**Conflict Summary:** <A brief, neutral summary of the conflict>
**Decision:** <The final decision>
**Rationale:** <The reason for the decision>
**Impact:** <The expected impact of the decision>
---
```
