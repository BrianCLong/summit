# AI Supply Chain Firewall Runbook

## Overview
This runbook guides operators on how to handle alerts generated by the AI Supply Chain Firewall.

## Interpretation of Risk
* **Slopsquatting:** Hallucinated packages or near-misses.
* **Typosquatting:** Edit distance similarity to popular packages.
* **Propagation Risk:** High recommendation velocity of new/untrusted packages.

## Approval Process
To waive a block, a justified approval must be submitted outlining why the package is safe.

## Alert Handling
* **Alert:** `ai_supply_chain_high_risk_dependency_blocked`
* **Action:** Review the deterministic evidence (`report.json`) to understand the block reason.
