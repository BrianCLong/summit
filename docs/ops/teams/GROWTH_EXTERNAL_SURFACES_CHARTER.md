# Charter: Growth & External Surfaces Team

**Owner:** `growth-external-surfaces-team`
**Escalation:** `vp-of-marketing`

## 1. Mission

To manage the public perception and external-facing surfaces of the Summit platform. This team is responsible for all public claims, documentation, and communication, ensuring that our messaging is accurate, consistent, and aligned with our product strategy.

## 2. Owned Surfaces

This team has primary ownership of the following repository paths and artifacts:

- **/website/**: The public-facing marketing and documentation website.
- **/docs/**: All public-facing documentation, including user guides, API references, and tutorials.
- **All press releases, blog posts, and other public announcements**.
- **The Public Claim Ledger**.
- **Drift guard automation** for public-facing surfaces.

## 3. Required Artifacts

The Growth & External Surfaces Team is required to produce and maintain the following artifacts:

- **Public Claim Ledger:** A verifiable record of all public claims made about the Summit platform, with pointers to the evidence that substantiates them.
- **Drift Guard Reports:** Regular reports from automated tooling that detects any drift between public claims and the underlying reality of the platform.
- **Content Calendar:** A schedule of all planned public communications.

## 4. GA Responsibilities

For the external surfaces to be considered "ready" for a GA release, the Growth & External Surfaces Team must:

- **Update all public documentation** to reflect the new features and changes in the release.
- **Verify that all public claims** are accurate and substantiated by evidence from the other teams.
- **Prepare all public communication materials**, including blog posts and press releases.
- **Provide a formal attestation** of readiness for the Go/No-Go packet.

## 5. Guardrails (What This Team May NOT Change Unilaterally)

The Growth & External Surfaces Team must not:

- **Make any public claims** about the platform that are not substantiated by verifiable evidence.
- **Publish any public communication** without approval from the relevant stakeholders (e.g., Product, Legal).

## 6. Escalation Path

- For disagreements with other teams regarding public claims, escalate to the **VP of Marketing**.
- For legal or compliance concerns, escalate to the **Legal Department**.
All escalations must be documented in the [Decision Log](../DECISION_LOG.md).
