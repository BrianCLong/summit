# Summit Execution Sprint Plan

## THE TOP 5 OUTCOMES (SUMMIT-DAY MUST-HAVES)

To be considered a strategic success, these five metrics must be achieved by EOD on Summit Day (T+0):

1. **Revenue Impact**: **>50 net-new Enterprise pipeline meetings booked.** (Indicator: CRM meeting count tagged to the Summit campaign).
2. **Trust / Brand Positioning**: **>85% positive sentiment with 0 critical negative PR cycles.** (Indicator: Social listening aggregate score on launch announcements).
3. **Operational Execution**: **99.99% uptime on all demo environments, streams, and registration portals.** (Indicator: 0 Sev-0/Sev-1 incidents during the 6-hour peak window).
4. **Risk Containment**: **100% of executives deliver legally-cleared, strict-to-script messaging.** (Indicator: Zero required post-event retractions, clarifications, or rogue feature announcements).
5. **Post-Summit Leverage**: **100% of attendees securely routed into targeted Day+1 sales sequences.** (Indicator: Automated marketing enrollment success rate).

---

## EXECUTION MODES

Before committing resources, we must align on the risk/reward posture:

*   **Safe Mode**: Pre-recorded keynotes, minimal live elements, targeted heavily at existing customers. *(Low risk, lower virality)*
*   **Standard Mode: Hybrid execution—live keynotes and Q&A, pre-recorded technical deep dives, synchronized PR bursts, targeted VIP networking.** *(Balanced risk/reward)* — **[DEFAULT RECOMMENDATION]**
*   **Aggressive Mode**: Fully live un-netted demos, open-mic Q&A, direct competitor call-outs, immediate public Beta access. *(High risk, high virality - requires Executive Override)*

## 30-DAY POST-SUMMIT COMPOUNDING PLAN
*The event is the starting line. Prioritize durable leverage.*

*   **T+1 to T+3 (The Strike):**
    *   Deploy role-specific recap emails (Technical vs. Executive summaries).
    *   Sales reps execute 1:1 outreach to the top 20% of highly-engaged attendees.
    *   Publish the "Definitive Guide to [Topic]" summarizing the keynote.
*   **T+7 to T+14 (The Deepening):**
    *   Chop the 1-hour keynote into 15 micro-videos for organic social distribution.
    *   Host a gated "Technical Deep Dive" webinar for practitioners who wanted more than the high-level pitch.
    *   Release updated Competitive Battlecards to the sales team based on competitor reactions.
*   **T+15 to T+30 (The Harvest):**
    *   Launch targeted paid retargeting ads to all attendees who haven't booked a meeting.
    *   Publish a case study highlighting an Alpha/Beta user of the newly launched product.
    *   **Exec Review:** Analyze Pipeline/Cost ratio. Document lessons learned for the next cycle.

## FALLBACK SCENARIO: "DARK MODE" (If everything goes wrong)
If the streaming platform collapses, the demo environment is breached, or a massive macro-event eclipses the news cycle:
1.  **Immediate Action:** Pivot to asynchronous. Shut down live feeds.
2.  **Comms:** Deploy pre-approved "Technical Difficulties / Respecting the News Cycle" email to all registrants.
3.  **Delivery:** Switch the main landing page to host the Pre-Recorded Video assets immediately.
4.  **Sales:** Pivot SDRs from "Booking Meetings" to "Delivering Value" by sending direct links to the assets with a personalized apology/note.
