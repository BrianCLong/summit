# Incident-to-Claims Matrix

This matrix maps potential security and operational incidents to the legal claims that plaintiffs or regulators are likely to allege.

| Incident Type                            | Likely Legal Claims                                                                                                                                   | Jurisdiction Notes                                                                                                                                                                  |
| :--------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Data Breach (PII)**                    | - Negligence<br>- Breach of Contract<br>- State Consumer Protection Acts (e.g., CCPA/CPRA, UDAP)<br>- Unjust Enrichment                               | **US (General):** 50-state breach notification laws triggered.<br>**CA:** CCPA statutory damages for non-encrypted data.<br>**EU:** GDPR Art. 82 (material & non-material damages). |
| **Data Breach (PHI)**                    | - HIPAA Violation (Statutory - rare private right of action, usually negligence per se)<br>- State Medical Privacy Laws<br>- Breach of Fiduciary Duty | **US:** HIPAA sets standard of care. State laws may allow private lawsuits.                                                                                                         |
| **Ransomware / Downtime**                | - Breach of Contract (SLA violation)<br>- Negligence (Failure to secure)<br>- Business Interruption claims                                            | **B2B:** Liability often capped by contract (check exceptions).<br>**Critical Infra:** Higher scrutiny.                                                                             |
| **Biometric Data Leak**                  | - BIPA (IL) violations<br>- CUBI (TX) violations<br>- Statutory privacy violations                                                                    | **IL:** BIPA is strict liability with statutory damages ($1k-$5k per violation). Very high risk.                                                                                    |
| **Marketing / Sales Misrep**             | - Fraud / Fraudulent Inducement<br>- Breach of Express Warranty<br>- False Advertising (Lanham Act, State laws)                                       | Claims often based on "Best-in-class security" or "Bank-grade encryption" statements found to be untrue.                                                                            |
| **Employee Insider Threat**              | - Negligent Hiring / Supervision<br>- Respondeat Superior (Vicarious Liability)                                                                       | Employer often liable for employee actions within scope of employment.                                                                                                              |
| **Vendor/Subprocessor Breach**           | - Breach of Contract (if liable for subs)<br>- Negligence (in selection/oversight)                                                                    | **GDPR:** Controller liable for Processor actions unless due diligence proved.                                                                                                      |
| **Logging Failure (No Evidence)**        | - Spoliation of Evidence (in litigation)<br>- Regulatory Fines (GDPR/HIPAA require accountability)                                                    | Missing logs makes defense against negligence claims extremely difficult.                                                                                                           |
| **Unauthorized Export (Data Residency)** | - GDPR Transfer Violation (Schrems II)<br>- Breach of Contract (Data Locality clauses)                                                                | **EU-US:** High risk if reliance on invalidated mechanisms (Privacy Shield).                                                                                                        |
