# Exposure Map & Claim Model (Epic 1)

This directory contains artifacts related to the "Exposure Map & Claim Model" epic, which focuses on identifying and mapping legal and financial exposure risks.

## Contents

- `incident-claims-matrix.md`: Mapping of incidents to potential legal claims.
- `data-inventory.md`: Inventory of processed data types and legal regimes.
- `litigation-readiness.md`: Checklist for litigation readiness.
- `financial-exposure-model.md`: Model for calculating financial exposure.
- `repeat-risk-vectors.md`: Analysis of repeat-risk vectors.
