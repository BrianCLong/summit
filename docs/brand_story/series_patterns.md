# Series Patterns

Common patterns for episodic personal branding.

## The Mini-Series

A 5-episode arc focusing on a single theme or "defining moment".

- Episode 1: The Beginning (The Hook)
- Episode 2: The Struggle (The Tension)
- Episode 3: The Turning Point (The Twist)
- Episode 4: The Result (The Lesson)
- Episode 5: The Future (The Vision)

## Themed Weeks

Thematic consistency across multiple posts to drive deep engagement on a specific topic.
