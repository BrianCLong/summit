# Brand Storytelling Primitives

Derived from common storytelling patterns and industry best practices.

## Episode Schema (The Arc)

Every episode in a brand story series should follow this arc:

1. **Hook**: Grab attention with a surprising or relatable opening.
2. **Tension**: Introduce the obstacle or challenge faced.
3. **Twist**: A shift in perspective or an unexpected turn.
4. **Lesson**: The core takeaway aligned to the brand mission.
5. **CTA**: A clear call to action or engagement prompt.
6. **Cliffhanger**: Anticipation for the next episode.

## Engagement Hooks

- **Interactive Prompts**: Polls, votes, or questions to involve the audience.
- **Vulnerability Markers**: Authentic sharing of lessons learned from failures.
- **Authority Markers**: Demonstrating expertise through shared experience.
