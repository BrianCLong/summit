# Audience-Specific Narrative Briefs (Aligned, Non-Escalating)

Each audience hears a **different emphasis** of the same factual core.

---

## Customers

### Operators

- Summit helps you **organize evidence and relationships** with traceable sources.
- You stay accountable for decisions; Summit preserves the audit trail.

### Risk owners

- Summit reduces risk by enforcing policy-as-code and preserving lineage.
- Governance controls are **embedded**, not optional.

### Executives

- Summit strengthens defensible analysis without automating decisions.
- It scales trust by making evidence and accountability visible.

---

## Sales & GTM

- Position Summit as **evidence-governed intelligence analysis**.
- Lead with provenance, governance, and human-in-control design.
- Avoid claims implying autonomy or guaranteed outcomes.

---

## Partners & Integrators

- Summit is a governed platform for **traceable analysis workflows**.
- Integrations must preserve provenance and pass policy checks.
- Partnership value is in compliant, auditable extensions.

---

## Auditors & Regulators

- Summit provides **audit-ready lineage** and policy-as-code enforcement.
- System does not decide or execute; human accountability is explicit.
- Evidence preservation and reviewability are core guarantees.

---

## Internal Teams

- Narrative must map to product guarantees and UI contracts.
- Any new capability must be justified by **evidence** and **policy coverage**.
- Over-claiming is a defect; honest constraints are a differentiator.
