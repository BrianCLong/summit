## Fields
- **pack_id**: Unique identifier for the brief.
- **title**: Title of the brief.
- **timeframe**: Period covered by the brief.
- **ranked_items**: List of narrative items sorted by risk/importance.
- **key_takeaways**: Deterministic summary points.
- **recommended_next_steps**: Allowed defensive actions.
- **confidence_notes**: Notes on data quality and confidence.
- **audit**: Audit trail of the generation process.
