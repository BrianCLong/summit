# IC Analyst Training Curriculum

## Overview
This curriculum is designed to certify Intelligence Community analysts as **Summit Certified Intelligence Operators (SCIO)**. The program consists of 40+ hours of instruction, blending theory, hands-on labs, and mission simulations.

## Module 1: Foundations (8 Hours)
*   **101: Introduction to Graph Intelligence:** Moving from tabular thinking to network thinking.
*   **102: Summit Interface Navigation:** Mastering the Triple Pane (Map, Graph, Timeline).
*   **103: Search & Discovery:** Advanced querying using natural language and structured filters.
*   **104: Workspace Management:** Organizing investigations, saving views, and collaborating in real-time.

## Module 2: Tradecraft & Analysis (16 Hours)
*   **201: Entity Resolution Workshop:** Techniques for merging and de-conflicting diverse datasets.
*   **202: Pattern of Life Analysis:** Using temporal analysis to detect anomalies in target behavior.
*   **203: Network Analysis:** Applying centrality, community detection, and pathfinding algorithms to uncover hidden networks.
*   **204: Geospatial Fusion:** Integrating imagery and SIGINT for precise location intelligence.

## Module 3: Advanced Agentic Workflows (8 Hours)
*   **301: Configuring Agents:** Setting up "Theorist" and "Experimentalist" agents for automated monitoring.
*   **302: Human-on-the-Loop:** Managing agent tasks, reviewing provenance, and correcting hallucinations.
*   **303: Influence Operations Analysis:** Mapping and countering disinformation campaigns.

## Module 4: Reporting & Dissemination (8 Hours)
*   **401: Multimedia Reporting:** Creating interactive reports with embedded graphs and timelines.
*   **402: Source Hygiene & Provenance:** Verifying evidence chains and applying proper classification markings.
*   **403: Capstone Exercise:** A full-day simulated crisis scenario requiring end-to-end use of the platform.
