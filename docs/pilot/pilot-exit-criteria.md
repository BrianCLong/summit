### Pilot Completion Conditions

The pilot is deemed **complete** when all conditions below are met:

### A. Operational Criteria

* ≥ 1 real incident governed end-to-end
* ≥ 1 decision refusal exercised
* ≥ 1 override with justification logged

### B. Evidence Criteria

* ≥ 95% decisions with complete evidence
* All decisions replayable
* No undocumented actions

### C. Governance Criteria

* Authority boundaries respected
* No silent overrides
* Refusal paths exercised without penalty

### D. Legal & Risk Criteria

* No policy violations attributable to Summit
* No increase in regulatory exposure
* Legal review of refusal records completed

### E. Executive Criteria

* Written executive assessment completed
* Expansion decision recorded:

  * Approve expansion
  * Approve limited extension
  * Terminate pilot

### Termination Clause

If exit criteria are not met, Summit:

* Is removed cleanly
* Leaves behind evidence artifacts
* Does not create dependency without approval

This protects **both parties**.
