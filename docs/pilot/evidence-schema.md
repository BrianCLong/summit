### Mandatory Evidence Fields (Per Decision)

- Decision ID
- Timestamp
- Decision Owner
- Evidence Sources (IDs + provenance)
- Confidence Bounds (numeric or ordinal)
- Policy Constraints Applied
- Alternative Actions Considered
- Outcome (Approved / Refused / Deferred)

### Evidence Standards

- Evidence must be time-bound
- Evidence must be attributable
- Evidence must be reproducible

**Missing evidence = automatic refusal**
