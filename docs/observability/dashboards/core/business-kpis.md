# Business KPIs Dashboard Spec (Placeholder)

**Goal:** Visualizes domain-specific success metrics. This dashboard should be tailored per service.

## Common Patterns

### 1. Key Business Events (Counter)

- **Examples:** `orders_placed`, `signups_completed`, `reports_generated`.
- **Visualization:** Rate (per minute) and Total (counter).

### 2. Funnel Conversion Rates

- **Examples:** Signup Step 1 -> Step 2 -> Completion.
- **Visualization:** Bar chart or Stat panel with %.

### 3. Business Value Volume

- **Examples:** Total transaction value ($), Data ingested (GB).
