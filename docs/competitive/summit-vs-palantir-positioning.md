# Competitive Positioning: Summit vs Palantir

## Palantir Strengths
- Mature platform.
- Deep government penetration.
- Powerful data fusion.

## Palantir Constraints
- Black-box analytics for many end users.
- High cost and long deployment cycles.
- Limited transparency in evidence workflows.
- Perceived vendor lock-in.

## Summit Advantages
1. **Evidence-First AI**: Every claim is provable.
2. **Governance-Native**: Policy enforcement is built in.
3. **Agentic Automation**: Multiplies analysts with governed automation.
4. **Open and Modular**: Deployable across environments without hard lock-in.
5. **Cost-Aware Intelligence**: Model routing and efficiency built in.

## Positioning Statement
Summit is an evidence-native intelligence platform combining agentic AI, verifiable insights, and governance-by-design to deliver trustable intelligence at a lower operating cost.
