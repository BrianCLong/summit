# Next.js Framework Governance Risk Standard

## Overview
This standard defines the governance risk profile for projects building upon the Next.js framework within the Summit ecosystem. It explicitly does not evaluate the technical quality of the framework but assesses the strategic dependency risk arising from ecosystem concentration, infrastructure coupling, and governance volatility.

## Purpose
To ensure that projects utilizing Next.js are explicitly evaluated for dependency concentration risk and hosting lock-in potential during the CI pipeline, via deterministic signals.

## Evaluation Criteria
- **Governance Opacity**: Measures transparency and predictability of core framework decisions.
- **Infra Coupling**: Measures the degree of alignment/lock-in between framework features and specific hosting environments.
- **Ecosystem Concentration**: Evaluates the dominance of the framework within its ecosystem and resulting centralization of influence.
- **Trust Volatility**: Tracks community trust metrics related to recent governance shifts.
