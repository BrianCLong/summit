# Text Intelligence Standard

## 1. Overview
The Text Intelligence Engine is designed to extract actionable insights from unstructured text documents.

## 2. Core Principles
- **Extract signals from huge document sets**: Turn qualitative info into quantitative metrics.
- **Track change over time**: Measure how narratives and risks change.
- **Deterministic output**: Ensure fully machine-verifiable metrics.

## 3. Evidence Tracking
All structured outputs must get a unique `evidence_id`.
