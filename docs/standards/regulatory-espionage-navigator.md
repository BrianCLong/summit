# Regulatory Espionage Navigator (REN) Standards

## Overview
REN models public-disclosure regimes as an espionage surface.

## Canonical Formats
- **RACF**: Regulatory Artifact Canonical Format.
- **RYVF**: Regulatory Yield Vector Format.
- **DEGF**: Disclosure Equilibrium Finding.

## Interop
REN exports signals to other Summit modules via `DEGF` output.
