# FireRed-OCR Item Mapping

Summit maps FireRed-OCR lessons into deterministic validators for structural correctness.

## Claim-scoped mapping

- Structural hallucinations are addressed through explicit structure contracts and fail-fast validators.
- Format-constrained reward shaping is represented as a future hook surface, not training-time dependence.
- Benchmark claims are treated as time-scoped and source-scoped, not absolute.

## Non-goals

- Summit does not train or ship OCR models in this framework.
- Summit does not claim benchmark leadership from this integration.
