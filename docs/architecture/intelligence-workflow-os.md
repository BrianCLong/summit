# Intelligence Workflow OS

This document defines the investigation lifecycle model and core workflow objects for Summit.

## Core Objects
- **Investigation**: The top-level container for an analytical mission.
- **Task**: A specific unit of work assigned to an agent or analyst.
- **Agent**: An autonomous or semi-autonomous actor performing work.
- **Evidence**: Verifiable artifacts that support or refute hypotheses.
- **ReviewState**: The current governance and quality status of a workflow.
- **DecisionRecord**: A formal record of a human or agent decision.

## Lifecycle Phases
1. **Signal**: Detection of a significant event or pattern.
2. **Investigation**: Formal opening of a case and objective setting.
3. **Hypothesis**: Formulation of competing theories.
4. **Evidence Validation**: Collection and verification of evidence artifacts.
5. **Analyst Review**: Human-in-the-loop review of findings.
6. **Report**: Final intelligence product and decision support.
