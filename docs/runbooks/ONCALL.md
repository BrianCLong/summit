# On-Call Runbook

## Responsibilities
- Acknowledge alerts within 5 minutes.
- Lead triage and mitigation for active incidents.
- Communicate status updates to stakeholders every 30 minutes during a SEV1/SEV2.

## Escalation Path
1. Primary On-Call (Level 1)
2. Secondary On-Call (Level 2)
3. Engineering Manager / Domain Expert (Level 3)
