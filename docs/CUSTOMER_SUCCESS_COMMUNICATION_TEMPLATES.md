                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    ## Template Best Practices

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    ### Usage Guidelines

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    1. **Personalization First**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    2.    - Always replace [PLACEHOLDER] with specific customer data
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          -    - Reference recent conversations and achievements
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               -    - Use customer's preferred communication style

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - 2. **Timing Matters**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      3.    - Send renewal emails 90-120 days before renewal
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            -    - Send health check-ins at calendar quarter endings
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 -    - Send escalations within 30 minutes of issue discovery

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - 3. **Tone and Professionalism**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        4.    - Remain customer-focused, not product-focused
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              -    - Celebrate their achievements, not ours
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   -    - Balance friendly and professional
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        -    - Always proofread before sending

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - 4. **Data Accuracy**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               5.    - Verify all metrics and dates
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     -    - Use actual customer metrics, not assumptions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          -    - Link to specific documentation/resources
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               -    - Confirm all contact names and titles

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - 5. **Follow-up Discipline**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      6.    - Track all send times and responses
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            -    - Set reminders for follow-ups (3, 7, 14 days)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 -    - Document all conversations
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      -    - Update CRM immediately after sending
