# Crisis Communications "Go-Bag"

**Purpose:** A pre-approved kit of messaging and protocols to control the narrative during the first 60 minutes and 60 days of a crisis.

**Owner:** Head of Communications
**Access:** Executive Staff, Legal, Comms Lead.

---

## 1. Immediate Response Protocol (First 60 Minutes)

1.  **Assess Severity:**
    *   **Level 1 (Internal/Minor):** Service glitch, handled by support.
    *   **Level 2 (Public/Moderate):** Visible outage, bad press.
    *   **Level 3 (Existential/Crisis):** Data breach, executive misconduct, lawsuit, regulatory raid.

2.  **Activate War Room:** (Level 2/3)
    *   **Attendees:** CEO, Legal, Comms, Tech/Subject Lead.
    *   **Goal:** Establish facts, determining privilege, approve initial holding statement.

3.  **Lockdown:**
    *   Pause all scheduled social media posts.
    *   Pause marketing emails.
    *   Remind employees: "Refer all media inquiries to [Comms Email]. Do not post on social media."

---

## 2. Escalation Tree (Who Decides / Who Speaks)

| Role | Responsibility | Backup |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Crisis Commander** | CEO (Strategy & Final Approval) | COO |
| **Narrative Lead** | Head of Comms (Drafting & Media) | Marketing VP |
| **Legal Guard** | General Counsel (Liability Check) | Outside Counsel |
| **Fact Owner** | CTO/CISO (Technical Truth) | VP Eng |
| **Spokesperson** | CEO (or designated trained exec) | Head of Comms |

## 3. Holding Statements (Fill-in-the-Blanks)

*Do not speculate. Admit what you know, state what you are doing, promise an update.*

### Scenario A: System Outage
> "We are currently experiencing service interruptions affecting [Product]. Our engineering team is actively diagnosing the issue to restore service as quickly as possible. We sincerely apologize for the disruption. Please verify status at [Status Page URL]."

### Scenario B: Security Incident (Unconfirmed)
> "We are aware of reports regarding a potential security incident. We have launched an immediate investigation to verify these claims. Security and privacy are our top priorities. We will share verified information as soon as it becomes available."

### Scenario C: Security Incident (Confirmed Data Breach)
> "We have detected unauthorized access to [System]. We immediately secured the environment and launched a forensic investigation. We are notifying affected customers and relevant authorities in accordance with our obligations. We are committed to transparency and will provide updates at [URL]."

### Scenario D: Leadership/Personnel Issue
> "We take allegations of misconduct seriously. We are conducting a thorough internal review in accordance with our company policies/values. As this is a personnel matter, we cannot comment further at this time."

---

## 4. Media Q&A (Defensive)

**Q: Was this a targeted attack?**
*A: "Our investigation is ongoing. We are focusing on remediation and determining the root cause."*

**Q: How many users are affected?**
*A: "We are currently sizing the impact. We will notify affected users directly."*

**Q: Will you compensate customers?**
*A: "Our priority right now is restoring service/security. We will address customer impact discussions once the situation is resolved."*

---

## 5. Employee Guidance

**Do:**
*   Focus on your work.
*   Report suspicious activity to Security.
*   Forward media/social inquiries to Comms.

**Don't:**
*   Speculate in Slack/Email (it is discoverable).
*   Engage with trolls or journalists on social media.
*   Share internal updates externally.
