# Fast Escalation Lane

## Purpose
To unblock pilot customers quickly and ensure their issues bypass standard Level 1 support queues.

## Workflow
1. **Submission**: Customer reports issue via dedicated Slack channel `#pilot-{customer}` or `pilot-support@company.com`.
2. **Triage**: Ticket is automatically tagged `pilot-blocker`.
3. **Routing**: Auto-routed to Engineering Lead and Pilot Support Lead via PagerDuty/Jira.
4. **SLA**:
    - **Response**: < 4 hours (business hours).
    - **Resolution Plan**: < 24 hours for blockers.
    - **Updates**: Every 4 hours for blockers.

## Roles
- **Pilot Support Lead**: Owns communication.
- **Eng Lead**: Owns technical resolution.
