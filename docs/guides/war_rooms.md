# War Room User Guide

This guide explains how to create and manage War Rooms in the IntelGraph platform.

## Creating a War Room

1. From the main dashboard, click the "Create War Room" button.
2. A modal window will appear, prompting you to enter a name for the War Room.
3. Enter a descriptive name and click "Create".
4. The new War Room will be created, and you will be automatically added as an administrator.

## Managing Participants

Once a War Room is created, you can add and remove participants.

### Adding Participants

1. Open the War Room you want to manage.
2. Click on the "Participants" tab.
3. Use the search bar to find users by name or email.
4. Select the users you want to add and assign them a role from the dropdown menu.
5. Click "Add Participants" to add them to the War Room.

### Participant Roles

* **Admin**: Can add and remove participants, assign roles, and delete the War Room.
* **Moderator**: Can add and remove participants.
* **Participant**: Can view and interact with the contents of the War Room.

### Removing Participants

1. Open the War Room you want to manage.
2. Click on the "Participants" tab.
3. Find the participant you want to remove in the list.
4. Click the "Remove" button next to their name.
