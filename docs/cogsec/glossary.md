# Cognitive Security Glossary

*   **Cognitive Incident**: An event involving manipulated information (disinformation, deepfakes) targeting the organization's reputation, executives, or assets.
*   **Cognitive Alert**: A signal indicating a potential cognitive incident, requiring triage.
*   **Fusion Cell**: A cross-functional team (Risk, Comms, Legal, Cyber) assembled to respond to incidents.
*   **Evidence Pack**: A standardized bundle of artifacts (`report.json`, `metrics.json`, `stamp.json`) proving the incident details and response actions.
*   **Decision Log**: A record of key decisions made during an incident, including rationale and approvers.
*   **Strategic Silence**: A decision to deliberately withhold public comment to avoid amplifying a false narrative.
*   **Rights Impact Assessment**: A review process to ensure response actions do not violate civil liberties or privacy rights.
*   **Deny-by-Default**: The security posture where actions are blocked unless explicitly allowed by policy (e.g., no profiling).
*   **Holding Statement**: A pre-approved, neutral statement acknowledging an issue without validating false details.
