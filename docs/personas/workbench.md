# Persona Risk & Visibility Workbench

## Commander's Intent
Provide a minimal Persona Risk & Visibility Workbench via the Summit CLI. This allows analysts to view and understand how well adversarial personas are linked across platforms and explicitly surfaces where deception and anti-linkage tactics create blind spots.

## Usage
Run the CLI inspect command for a specific persona:
`summit personas inspect --persona-id persona_123`

This produces a read-only terminal summary with:
- **Persona Summary:** General IDs, threat risk profiles, and active deception tactics.
- **Visibility & Risk Indicators:** Analytical scoring showing linkage coverage and deception pressure.
- **Fusion & Deception Explanation:** Raw breakdown of the evidence supporting linkage vs. evidence contradicting linkage.

## Abuse Analysis
- **Misuse Risk:** Workbench could be used by users to mutate or confirm linkages incorrectly, acting as an identity resolution tool.
- **Design Constraint:** The workbench command is strictly **read-only**, displaying hypotheses already generated by the internal engine. Analysts cannot use this surface to manually link human identities.
