                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           ## 7. Decision-Making Framework

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           ### 7.1 Decision Types

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           **Level 1 - Individual** (Developer can decide)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Code style and organization
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Test approach
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Local optimization
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - No stakeholder impact

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           **Level 2 - Team** (Team lead decides)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Architecture within service
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Framework choices
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Internal API changes
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Library updates

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           **Level 3 - Domain** (Domain lead decides)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Service boundaries
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Cross-service contracts
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - External dependencies
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Security implications

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           **Level 4 - Technical Leadership** (Tech steering decides)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Technology platform changes
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Major architecture shifts
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Third-party vendor selection
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Large budget items

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           **Level 5 - Executive** (Steering committee decides)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Product direction changes
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Major timeline adjustments
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Budget > $100K
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - Policy changes

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           ### 7.2 Decision Process

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           1. **Propose** (Issue, PR, design doc)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           2. **Discuss** (Comments, meetings, Slack)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           3. **Decide** (At appropriate level)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           4. **Document** (ADR or decision log)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           5. **Communicate** (Announce to stakeholders)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           6. **Execute** (Implement decision)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           7. **Review** (Retrospect and learn)
