# Positioning: Summit in the Execution Era

**Theme:** From Experimentation to Execution.
**Audience:** Intelligence Teams, Security Operations Centers (SOCs), Corporate Security.

## The Shift
The market has moved past the "wow" phase of Generative AI. 2026 is the year of **Execution**. Organizations are no longer asking "What can AI do?" but "How does this improve my KPIs *today*?"

## Summit's Unique Value Proposition (UVP)

### 1. The Multi-Agent Operating System for Intel
Summit is not an "LLM App". It is an **Operating System** that orchestrates specialized agents (Researchers, Analysts, Auditors) to collaborate on a shared knowledge graph (IntelGraph™).
*   **Differentiation:** Competitors offer "chat with data". Summit offers **"agents that work"**—proactively hunting, verifying, and connecting dots without constant human prompting.

### 2. Physical & Action-Oriented
Summit prepares you for the next wave: **Action**.
*   We don't just summarize text. Our agents interact with tools, query APIs, and can be extended to control physical systems (via "Physical AI" integrations) or interact with Digital Twins.

### 3. Radical Governance
We turn compliance into a feature.
*   **Determinism:** Our "Record & Replay" capability ensures that if an agent makes a decision today, you can reproduce exactly *why* and *how* it made that decision 6 months from now.
*   **Safety:** Built-in red-teaming and policy layers prevent hallucination-induced errors in critical workflows.

## Strategic Pillars for 2026

1.  **Metric-Driven:** Every feature must tie back to efficiency or accuracy metrics.
2.  **Hybrid-Native:** Cloud for speed, Self-Hosted (SLM/Mid-tier) for security and cost.
3.  **Agent-Identity:** Trust the agent because you can verify its identity and history.
