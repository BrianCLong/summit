# Ecosystem Tier 1: Insight Providers

**Status:** Safest, Broadest
**Rights:** Read-only value creation

## Who Belongs Here

### Archetype 1: Threat & Risk Intelligence Providers

- Threat intel feeds
- Fraud risk scorers
- Insider risk indicators
- Data enrichment providers

## What They Can Do

- Provide signals
- Add annotations
- Offer contextual metadata
- Classify source reliability

## What They Cannot Do

- Influence decisions directly
- Assert confidence beyond bounds
- Override decay or revocation rules
- Frame urgency or use prescriptive action language

## Integration Mechanism

**Information Supply Chain Adapters**

- **Explicit Source Classification**: Every piece of data must have a clear origin.
- **Confidence Bounds**: Providers must state their confidence (e.g., "Medium", "80%").
- **Automatic Decay**: Signals must have a time-to-live (TTL) or relevance decay function.

## Economic Effect

- Large ecosystem surface
- Low governance risk
- Easy partner onboarding
