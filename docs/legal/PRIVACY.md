# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** October 2025

## 1. Information We Collect

1.1. **Account Information**: We collect email addresses and names for authentication and account management.
1.2. **Usage Data**: We collect telemetry data regarding how you use the platform (e.g., API calls, graph query performance) to improve system stability and performance.
1.3. **AI Interactions**: We process your inputs to LLM features (prompts) to generate responses. These inputs are not retained for model training unless explicitly opted-in.

## 2. How We Use Information

2.1. **Service Provision**: To provide, maintain, and improve the Summit platform.
2.2. **Security**: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
2.3. **Communication**: To send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.

## 3. Data Sharing

3.1. **Service Providers**: We may share data with third-party vendors (Subprocessors) who provide services on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting, LLM providers).
3.2. **Legal Compliance**: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data. Contact `privacy@summit.ai` to exercise these rights.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. Please refer to our [Data Retention Policy](./DATA_RETENTION.md) for specifics.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at `privacy@summit.ai`.
