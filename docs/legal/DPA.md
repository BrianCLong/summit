# Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

**Last Updated:** October 2025

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Service between you ("Customer") and Summit ("Provider").

## 1. Scope

This DPA applies to the processing of Personal Data by Provider on behalf of Customer in the course of providing the Services.

## 2. Roles

2.1. **Customer** is the Controller of Customer Personal Data.
2.2. **Provider** is the Processor of Customer Personal Data.

## 3. Provider Obligations

3.1. **Instructions**: Provider shall process Customer Personal Data only on documented instructions from Customer.
3.2. **Confidentiality**: Provider shall ensure that persons authorized to process the Personal Data have committed themselves to confidentiality.
3.3. **Security**: Provider shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk.

## 4. Subprocessors

Provider engages third-party subprocessors to provide certain parts of the Services. A list of current subprocessors is available at [Subprocessors](./SUBPROCESSORS.md). Customer consents to the engagement of these subprocessors.

## 5. Data Subject Rights

Provider shall assist Customer, insofar as this is possible, for the fulfilment of the Customer's obligation to respond to requests for exercising the data subject's rights.

## 6. Audit

Provider shall make available to Customer all information necessary to demonstrate compliance with the obligations laid down in this DPA and allow for and contribute to audits, including inspections, conducted by Customer or another auditor mandated by Customer.
