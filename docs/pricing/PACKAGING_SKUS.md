# Summit Packaging & SKUs

## Deployment Modes (SKUs)

Summit is offered in three primary editions tailored to different organizational needs:

### 1. Internal Edition

- **Target**: Single organization, internal use.
- **Key Features**:
  - Fastest time-to-value.
  - Full platform observability.
  - Standard policy engine.
  - Native cost attribution.
- **Value Prop**: Consolidate and secure internal automations.

### 2. White-Label Edition

- **Target**: Partners, MSPs, and large enterprises providing services to sub-entities.
- **Key Features**:
  - Custom branding/theming.
  - Multi-tenant policy profiles.
  - Connector certification kit.
  - Offline upgrade playbooks for air-gapped sub-tenants.
- **Value Prop**: Deliver trustworthy automation as a service.

### 3. Hosted SaaS

- **Target**: Cloud-first organizations.
- **Key Features**:
  - Full tenant isolation.
  - Regional data residency controls.
  - Per-tenant metering & partner console.
  - Managed SLAs and maintenance.
- **Value Prop**: Zero-friction scale with enterprise-grade governance.

## Pricing Anchors

Pricing is designed to correlate with COGS and value drivers:

| Anchor               | Description                                           | Units                       |
| :------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------- |
| **Seats**            | Number of active users on Switchboard.                | Per User/Month              |
| **Action Runs**      | Individual tool/connector executions.                 | Per 1k Runs                 |
| **Workflow Runs**    | End-to-end durable workflow executions.               | Per 100 Runs                |
| **Ingest Units**     | Volume of events or entities ingested via connectors. | Per GB or 100k Entities     |
| **Evidence Bundles** | Creation and retention of signed evidence exports.    | Per Bundle / Retention Tier |

## Enterprise Add-ons

- **Advanced Data Residency**: Private networking and specific region pinning.
- **Dedicated Security**: Dedicated KMS / HSM integration.
- **Compliance Extended**: Advanced audit export and long-term retention policies.
- **Premium Support**: Dedicated TAM + priority connector development SLA.
