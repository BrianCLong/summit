## Evidence Governance (Use in both decks)

### Source Confidence Framework
- **Tier 1 (Highest confidence):** Primary research, regulatory text, public filings, audited reports.
- **Tier 2:** Reputable industry analysis and established media reporting.
- **Tier 3:** Vendor marketing pages, sponsored content, social posts.

### Rule for Claims in Final Decks
- Any market-size or performance claim on a slide must include a confidence marker and source tier in speaker notes.
- If a claim is Tier 3 only, mark it as directional and pair it with an internal benchmark or customer evidence.
- Prefer ranges over point estimates when source certainty is mixed.

### Minimum Appendix Pack
1. Market model + assumptions.
2. Competitive matrix methodology.
3. Technical benchmark protocol.
4. Pilot KPI definitions.
5. Compliance control mapping.
