## Segment Narratives

### Segment 1: Government / Defense / Intelligence

**Overview**: This segment represents our core heritage and highest-value opportunities. Agencies face mounting pressure to "connect the dots" across fragmented data sources while maintaining strict security and compliance standards. Recent high-profile intelligence failures and budget allocations toward AI/ML modernization create significant tailwinds.

**Key Insights**:
- **Buying Cycles**: 12-24 months from first contact to contract award; requires sustained engagement through RFI â†’ RFP â†’ evaluation â†’ award
- **Average Contract Value**: $2M-$50M (multi-year IDIQ)
- **Decision Drivers**: Security clearances, FedRAMP/IL-5 compliance, incumbent relationships, mission impact
- **Competitive Landscape**: Palantir (dominant incumbent), BAH/CACI (SI-led), legacy tools (i2 Analyst Notebook, Analyst's Notebook)

**Winning Strategy**:
1. **Lead with mission outcomes**: Faster threat detection, analyst force multiplier, zero compliance violations
2. **Proof points**: Pilot with friendly agency, veteran/cleared team, FedRAMP in progress
3. **Partnership model**: Teaming with Primes (Booz Allen, CACI) for large procurements, direct for smaller agencies
4. **Differentiation**: Deployability (air-gapped, edge), AI-augmented insights (vs. manual Palantir), modern UX

**Overview**: This segment punches above its weight in **mission alignment** and **brand value**. High-profile investigations (Panama Papers, Bellingcat) generate PR and attract talent.
