### Problem
Mission workflows are split across systems and compartments, creating delays, provenance gaps, and risk in sensitive operations.

### Outcomes
- Deliver verifiable intelligence outcomes with chain-of-custody provenance.
- Enforce compartmented policy controls without slowing operations.
- Maintain offline continuity with deterministic resync and audit integrity.

### Differentiators
- **Provenance-first disclosure:** Evidence is captured at every step, not after the fact.
- **Multi-tenant policy isolation:** Policy-enforced boundaries across mission lanes.
- **Offline case sync:** Field operability with secure resynchronization.
- **Governed exceptions:** Legacy constraints are handled as governed exceptions, not hidden workarounds.

### Proof

### Security Posture
