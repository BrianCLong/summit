# Summit Enterprise

**Scope:** The sovereignty and governance layer for at-scale deployment.
**Focus:** Security, compliance, auditability, and control.

## Capabilities
- **Sovereign Deployment:** Air-gapped, VPC, or On-premise installation options.
- **Cognitive Ops:** Governance engines that monitor and constrain agent behavior.
- **Audit Trails:** Immutable logs of every agent decision and action.
- **RBAC:** Fine-grained access control at the entity and action level.

## Compliance
- Designed for SOC2, GDPR, and EU AI Act compliance.
- See [Governance Docs](./GOVERNANCE.md) for policy definitions.
