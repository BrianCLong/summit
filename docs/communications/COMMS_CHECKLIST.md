# Communications Checklist

This checklist is a tool to ensure all outbound artifacts are compliant with the [Communications Playbook](./communications-playbook-execution-layer.md).

## Internal Comms

- [ ] Tone: Direct, Calm, Competent, Non-performative?
- [ ] Content: Clear priorities, honest constraints, directional timelines, succinct rationale?
- [ ] Prohibited Patterns: No public internal disagreement, speculation, emotional venting, or over-detailed future commitments?

## Press & Media

- [ ] Tone: Authoritative, Reserved, Precise, Quote-safe?
- [ ] Rules: Speaking only on what is true now, framing progress without timelines, no speculation?
- [ ] Posture: Calm confidence, willing silence, minimal adjectives, high signal density?

## Investor & Capital

- [ ] Tone: Serious, Long-term, Measured optimism, Data-backed?
- [ ] Required Elements: Clear strategic throughline, honest articulation of risk, evidence of learning velocity, capital efficiency narrative?
- [ ] Forbidden: No surprise disclosures, overly precise timelines, inflated TAM narratives, or reactive defensiveness?

## Talent & Reputation

- [ ] Tone: Exacting, Purposeful, Non-salesy, Respectful of candidate intelligence?
- [ ] Signaling Strategy: Standards over perks, mission clarity over culture hype, autonomy with accountability?
- [ ] Anti-Patterns: No "family" language, vague values, or overpromised growth paths?

## Marketing & Growth

- [ ] Tone: Confident, Clear, Outcome-oriented?
- [ ] Messaging Hierarchy: Problem gravity, outcome achieved, subtle differentiation, proof signals?
- [ ] Avoid: No feature dumping, trend chasing, or claims that lock roadmap prematurely?

## Advertising

- [ ] Principles: Assume audience intelligence, be concise and legible, one core message per asset?

## Thought Leadership & White Paper

- [ ] Standards: Technically defensible, citable, durable beyond news cycles?
- [ ] White Paper Requirements: Clear problem framing, novel contribution, explicit assumptions, reproducible reasoning?

## Bureaucratic & Regulatory

- [ ] Tone: Respectful, Process-aware, Documentation-forward?
- [ ] Strategy: Over-clarify intent, understate ambition, demonstrate compliance literacy?

## Crisis & Controversy

- [ ] Tone: Calm, Factual, Non-emotional?
- [ ] Absolute Rules: No public blame, no speculative causes, no rushed statements?

## Continuity & Memory

- [ ] Rules: Compatible with past statements, allows future evolution, avoids irreversible commitments, preserves optionality?
