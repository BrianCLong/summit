# Summit Target Accounts — Q1 2026 Priority Ranking + Outreach Scripts

## Purpose

This brief translates the 10 near-term target accounts into a **fit-ranked execution list** for Summit's current roadmap emphasis:

1. Agentic security + SOC governance
2. Policy-as-code + evidence bundles
3. Sovereign, graph-native AI operations
4. Public-sector readiness (FedRAMP/agency constraints)

## Fit Scoring Method

Each account is scored 1–5 across:

- **Roadmap fit**: alignment to Summit's immediate product surfaces
- **Buying urgency**: evidence of active budget, RFP, or public launch window
- **Champion density**: likelihood of finding a technical + budget champion pair
- **Channel reachability**: quality of channels available in the next 2 weeks
- **Deal leverage**: strategic value (logo, downstream references, repeatability)

Weighted score:

- Roadmap fit: 30%
- Buying urgency: 25%
- Champion density: 15%
- Channel reachability: 10%
- Deal leverage: 20%

## Qualification Gate (Use Before Scheduling Deep Dives)

A lead is qualified for technical deep-dive only if at least 3 criteria are true:

- Active AI or SOC governance initiative this quarter
- Named owner for security/compliance sign-off
- Need for model/agent interoperability (multi-vendor or multi-model)
- Audit/evidence requirement tied to procurement or internal controls
- POC window in next 30–60 days

## Forward-Leaning Enhancement (Recommended)

Implement a lightweight **"Evidence-Readiness Calculator"** in the sales workflow:

- Input: 10–12 control requirements from prospect (residency, identity, logging, policy coverage)
- Output: readiness score + control gap map + suggested pilot scope
- Value: creates a repeatable technical qualifier and accelerates conversion from discovery to POC.
