# Partner Certification

Partners build upon, integrate with, or resell the platform.

## 1. Partner Levels

### 1.1 Registered Partner
*   **Definition:** Listed in the directory.
*   **Reqs:** Business verification, signed NDA.

### 1.2 Certified Integrator
*   **Definition:** Proven ability to deploy and configure the platform correctly.
*   **Reqs:**
    *   2+ Certified Operators on staff.
    *   Successful deployment of a Reference Architecture.
    *   Passing grade on "Integration Correctness" technical interview.

### 1.3 Strategic Premier Partner
*   **Definition:** Co-selling and deep roadmap alignment.
*   **Reqs:**
    *   5+ Certified Operators.
    *   Dedicated support channel integration.
    *   Joint incident handling drills.

## 2. Technical Requirements

*   **Integration Correctness:** Must use stable public APIs (no private hacks).
*   **Upgrade Discipline:** Must demonstrate ability to keep customer deployments within N-1 versions.
*   **Incident Handling:** Must have a 24/7 response capability compatible with Platform support.

## 3. Evidence
*   Case studies.
*   Architecture reviews.
*   SLA performance reports.
