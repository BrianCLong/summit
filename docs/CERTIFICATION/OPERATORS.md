# Operator Certification

Trust in the platform includes trust in the humans operating it.

## 1. Roles & Requirements

### 1.1 Platform Operator
*   **Responsibility:** Day-to-day health, deployment, and maintenance.
*   **Certification Requirement:**
    *   **Exam:** "Platform Operations Fundamentals" (70% pass).
    *   **Evidence:** 50 hours of logged operation time (or simulation).
    *   **Identity:** Verified corporate identity.

### 1.2 Security Officer
*   **Responsibility:** Policy management, audit response, incident command.
*   **Certification Requirement:**
    *   **Exam:** "Platform Security & Governance" (85% pass).
    *   **Background Check:** Standard corporate background check (attested by HR).
    *   **Evidence:** Completion of incident response drill.

### 1.3 Autonomy Approver
*   **Responsibility:** Authorizing high-tier autonomous actions.
*   **Certification Requirement:**
    *   **Exam:** "AI Safety & Autonomy Ethics" (90% pass).
    *   **Evidence:** documented review of 10+ autonomous run plans.

## 2. Certification Process

1.  **Training:** Completion of standard learning paths.
2.  **Assessment:** Proctored or logged exam execution.
3.  **Badge Issuance:** Digital credential stored in the registry.

## 3. Validity & Renewal
*   Operator certifications are valid for **1 year**.
*   Renewal requires a refresher course and re-test.
