# 90-Day Lighthouse Capture Plan: Tier-1 Financial Services

**Owner:** Jules
**Target:** Tier-1 Global Bank, Large Insurer, or Fintech Under Scrutiny
**Objective:** Secure initial enterprise adoption by positioning Summit as a governable intelligence OS, displacing generic CTI scoring with deterministic, regulator-ready pipelines.
**Status:** Active

## Strategic Mandate
*   **Positioning:** Focus on power mapping, regulatory foresight, narrative control, and pre-emptive lawfare posture.
*   **Narrative Focus:** "Defensibility over Discovery." Summit provides the proof chain required by boards and regulators, augmenting existing data lakes (like Recorded Future) with rigorous governance.
*   **Operational Rules:** Adhere to the "DIRECTORATE K++ Victory Doctrine" (Risk Reduction > Speed > Cost > Elegance).

## Execution Timeline

### Phase 1: Days 1-30 (Reconnaissance & Pre-emptive Lawfare Posture)
**Goal:** Map the decision-making landscape, understand regulatory pressure points, and establish an initial narrative wedge.

*   **Power Mapping:** Identify key stakeholders beyond the SOC (e.g., CISO, Chief Risk Officer, Audit Committee Chair).
*   **Regulatory Foresight:** Analyze recent SEC/OCC/PRA fines or findings impacting the target. Tailor messaging to these specific gaps (e.g., "Failure to maintain auditable threat response records").
*   **Narrative Control:** Draft initial outreach emphasizing the "governance gap" in current CTI workflows. Do not attack their current feed vendor; attack their *process defensibility*.
*   **Initial Engagement:** Conduct executive briefings leveraging the "Proof Moat" narrative. Focus on explainability, reproducibility, and chain-of-custody.

### Phase 2: Days 31-60 (Technical Validation & Policy-as-Code Integration)
**Goal:** Demonstrate Summit's hybrid capabilities and policy-driven intelligence within the target's specific constraints.

*   **Hybrid Architecture Proof:** Deploy the local-first/VPC quickstart to demonstrate compliance with data residency and vendor risk requirements.
*   **Scoring Blueprint Customization:** Co-develop an initial set of scoring blueprints using FS-native language (e.g., "Regulatory Exposure Modifier", "Revenue-impact Asset Mapping").
*   **Governance OS Integration:** Showcase the integration of Summit's ABAC/OPA policies with their existing GRC platform (e.g., ServiceNow IR, Archer).
*   **Deterministic Pipeline Demo:** Run a historical scenario through Summit, producing a deterministic report hash and signed bundle to prove exact knowledge at a past point in time.

### Phase 3: Days 61-90 (Commercial Closing & Lighthouse Establishment)
**Goal:** Formalize the partnership, structure the initial rollout, and position the target as a lighthouse logo.

*   **Business Case Finalization:** Quantify the reduction in audit preparation time, compliance risk, and incident response uncertainty.
*   **Sovereign Deployment Agreement:** Finalize terms regarding telemetry control, local processing, and data locality.
*   **Initial Rollout Scope:** Target a high-visibility, high-risk operational area (e.g., fraud/carding monitoring, critical infrastructure tracking) for the first production implementation.
*   **Lighthouse Formalization:** Secure agreement for a joint case study focusing on the transformation from generic CTI to a "governable intelligence OS."

## Success Criteria
*   Signed agreement for initial Summit deployment within a targeted high-risk operational unit.
*   Validation of the "extend or replace RF" motion without triggering defensive posture from existing vendor relationships.
*   Establishment of reproducible pipelines and EVID-tagged provenance for the initial use case.
