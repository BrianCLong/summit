# Wave 4: Predictive + Cognitive Systems Prompts (Jules)

Ultra-advanced, resilience-first prompts designed for ethical, defensive deployments. Each module can be developed independently or combined into an integrated foresight fabric.

## 1. Epistemic Stability Forecast Grid™
- **Goal:** Track knowledge confidence over time, predict fragmentation thresholds, and prescribe reinforcement.
- **Signals:** source freshness/variance, citation density, consensus deltas, schema drift, replication rates, novelty spikes.
- **Modeling:** temporal Bayesian confidence curves, topic-coherence decay, shock/fragmentation stress tests.
- **Outputs:** stability scorecards, early-warning deltas, critical-fragmentation thresholds, reinforcement playbooks.
- **Interventions:** redundancy plans, canonicalization passes, evidence revalidation, expert-in-the-loop checkpoints, provenance tightening.

## 2. Global Narrative Vector Field Engine™
- **Goal:** Map narrative forces as vector fields to anticipate attraction, repulsion, or orbital dynamics between story clusters.
- **Signals:** sentiment/stance gradients, actor influence weights, media cadence, memetic velocity, policy levers, coordination intensity.
- **Modeling:** manifold embeddings + vector-field integration, potential-energy surfaces for convergence/divergence, perturbation sims for counterfactual pushes.
- **Outputs:** trajectory forecasts, likely convergence corridors, divergence warnings, leverage points for stabilization.
- **Interventions:** counter-narrative buffers, transparency drops, bridge-building frames, cadence tuning, pre-bunking assets.

## 3. Cross-Domain Predictive Influence Lattice™
- **Goal:** Detect weak ties where one domain influences another and forecast ripple effects across sectors.
- **Signals:** cross-citation graphs, supply/standards dependencies, regulation timelines, talent/stack overlaps, funding flows, incident co-occurrence.
- **Modeling:** lattice coupling tensors, Granger/causal graph overlays, stress-propagation Monte Carlo, sensitivity maps.
- **Outputs:** predicted cross-domain cascades, lead/lag charts, resilience hedges, portfolio of mitigations.
- **Interventions:** diversification buffers, dependency pruning, standards harmonization, scenario drills across coupled domains.

## 4. Organizational Cognitive Entropy Predictor™
- **Goal:** Forecast when informational, cultural, or strategic coherence decays.
- **Signals:** decision latency, conflicting OKRs, duplicative docs, meeting churn, shadow stacks, sentiment drift, leadership message variance.
- **Modeling:** entropy curves on comms/decision graphs, topic drift tracking, alignment decay half-life, chaos game simulations on process graphs.
- **Outputs:** entropy heatmaps, catalyst lists, time-to-decohere estimates, recovery paths.
- **Interventions:** charter refreshes, runway clarifications, RFC gating, single-source-of-truth sweeps, escalation hygiene, comms rhythm resets.

## 5. Predictive Strategic Equilibrium Modeler™
- **Goal:** Simulate how strategic actors move toward equilibrium or divergence.
- **Signals:** payoff shifts, constraint updates, signaling frequency, coalition changes, budget vectors, regulatory moves.
- **Modeling:** dynamic game sims with stochastic shocks, replicator dynamics for strategy share, equilibrium stability analysis, tremble/perturbation runs.
- **Outputs:** equilibrium likelihood bands, destabilizer lists, pivot trees, stabilizing strategy bundles.
- **Interventions:** commitment devices, credible signaling, option scaffolding, phased reciprocity, transparent guardrails.

## 6. Collective Insight Emergence Engine™
- **Goal:** Predict when groups produce breakthroughs and how to accelerate them.
- **Signals:** idea recombination rates, diversity indices, review-to-merge ratios, prototype half-life, cross-team bridge density, psychological safety proxies.
- **Modeling:** percolation thresholds on idea graphs, information diffusion with novelty gating, burst-detection, motif mining for insight precursors.
- **Outputs:** breakthrough window forecasts, catalyst maps, recommended team pairings, high-leverage contexts.
- **Interventions:** time-boxed sprints with exploration budget, rotating review panels, knowledge jams, low-friction prototyping lanes, reflective debriefs.

## 7. Information Shockwave Propagation Simulator™
- **Goal:** Model how sudden info events propagate and destabilize networks.
- **Signals:** topology fragility, edge trust weights, reticulation speed, moderation latency, anomaly scores, dependency criticality.
- **Modeling:** shock injection sims on multiplex graphs, attenuation/decay curves, contagion + rumor-stifling models, circuit-breaker what-ifs.
- **Outputs:** impact cones, decay half-lives, vulnerable subnetworks, buffer playbooks.
- **Interventions:** rate-limiters, staged disclosure, quorum checks, provenance proofs, fail-open vs fail-closed runbooks, rapid debunk kits.

## 8. Predictive Ethical Drift Analyzer™
- **Goal:** Forecast ethical norm shifts under stress or incentives.
- **Signals:** incentive gradients, exception rates, override frequency, audit findings, whistle signals, policy lag, stakeholder sentiment.
- **Modeling:** norm-drift state machines, pressure/stress-response curves, causal tracing of incentive misalignment, counterfactual guardrail tests.
- **Outputs:** drift risk zones, early-warning deltas, expected drift vectors, recalibration triggers.
- **Interventions:** incentive realignment, periodic ethics retros, red-team/blue-team drills, transparent exception registries, reinforcement training.

## 9. Meta-Conflict Trajectory Forecaster™
- **Goal:** Model conflicts between idea-systems or strategy frames (not people) and predict escalation/resolution arcs.
- **Signals:** claim incompatibility scores, resource overlap, narrative antagonism, reframing attempts, mediation density, shared-ground motifs.
- **Modeling:** collision simulations with payoff reshaping, narrative bargaining spaces, attractor basin mapping for resolution states.
- **Outputs:** escalation probability ladders, resolution pathways, de-escalation leverage points, mediator kits.
- **Interventions:** reframing playbooks, shared-ground synthesis, scoped pilot zones, mutual-verification channels, synchronized disclosure.

## 10. Societal Systems Harmonization Predictor™
- **Goal:** Forecast harmony/disharmony cycles across interacting economic, cultural, and informational systems.
- **Signals:** policy cadence, macro indicators, cultural sentiment indexes, supply/infra stress, media polarization, interoperability coverage.
- **Modeling:** coupled oscillator models for cross-system resonance, structural equation models, regime-switching detectors, stability basins under shocks.
- **Outputs:** harmony windows, disharmony risk maps, synchronization levers, coordinated stabilization routes.
- **Interventions:** cadence alignment, standards adoption campaigns, buffer stock triggers, joint comms frameworks, rapid reconciliation forums.

## 11. Predictive Value-Alignment Drift Map™
- **Goal:** Forecast value drift from mission baselines and map friction zones.
- **Signals:** decision traceability gaps, principle overrides, hiring mix shifts, partner pull, KPIs vs mission divergence, feedback loop integrity.
- **Modeling:** value alignment vectors with drift velocity, friction heatmaps, alignment half-life estimation, commitment-vs-practice deltas.
- **Outputs:** drift trajectories, friction zones, reinforcement paths, renewal checkpoints.
- **Interventions:** value refresh cycles, mission-indexed KPIs, boundary-setting rituals, partner alignment gates, reflective after-action reviews.

---
**Ethics & Safety Defaults:** privacy-first, harm minimization, transparency, human-in/on-the-loop controls, auditability, and stress-tested guardrails across all modules.
