## 📌 Practical Takeaways for Governing AI at Scale

**1. Centralize Visibility & Control**
Platforms offering **unified inventories and telemetry** (e.g., Microsoft Agent 365, Purview DSPM) reduce shadow AI risk and help enforce policy across heterogeneous AI systems. ([Microsoft][5])

**2. Embed Governance Early in the AI Lifecycle**
Lifecycle governance that integrates with development, deployment, and operations (e.g., Dataiku’s integrated governance) improves **audit readiness and compliance consistency**. ([Dataiku][8])

**3. Align with Regulatory Frameworks**
Frameworks like the **EU AI Act** and NIST AI RMF guide risk‑based governance practices, ensuring organizations implement controls proportional to AI risk. ([Wikipedia][10])

**4. Treat Governance as Strategic, Not Just Compliance**
Governance investments enhance **trust, scalability, and innovation velocity** by enabling responsible AI use while meeting regulatory and enterprise risk requirements. ([Computer Weekly][12])
