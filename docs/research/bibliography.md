# Research Bibliography: GraphRAG and Narrative Intelligence

## Metadata
- **Assumptions:** The papers listed herein are assumed to be representative of the state-of-the-art in their respective domains as of early 2024. The methodologies described are presumed applicable to the Summit architecture.
- **Limitations:** This bibliography is not exhaustive. It focuses primarily on publicly available literature (arXiv, ACL Anthology, IEEE, ACM DL, DARPA/IARPA public releases) and may exclude proprietary or classified research. The URLs provided may change over time.
- **Explicit Non-Claims:** This bibliography does not claim to provide a definitive solution to information operations detection, nor does it guarantee that the included papers' methodologies will succeed in every production environment without adaptation.
