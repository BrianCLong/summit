# Intelligence Brief: t54 Labs Seed Round

## Overview
**t54 Labs**, a San Francisco-based startup building "trust infrastructure" for autonomous AI agents that handle financial transactions, has raised **$5 million in a seed funding round**. The round was co-led by Anagram, PL Capital, and Franklin Templeton with strategic participation by Ripple.

## What t54 Labs Is Building
t54 Labs aims to create a **"trust layer" for the agentic economy** — where AI agents act autonomously in financial roles rather than just advising humans.

Their infrastructure includes:
* **Identity & verification** ("Know Your Agent") to bind agents to real identities and intents.
* **Real-time risk and fraud monitoring** to detect suspicious actions before money moves.
* **Agent-native credit systems** to underwrite borrowing based on agent behavior.
* **Unified platform layer** combining ID, risk controls, and settlement.

The company emphasizes using **blockchain as an auditable settlement and accountability layer**, and its platform already operates across multiple chains including **XRP Ledger, Solana, and Base**.

## Strategic Context

### Ripple & Franklin Templeton
* **Ripple's participation** signals its interest in positioning the XRP Ledger and related infrastructure as foundational rails for machine-to-machine and AI-enabled financial activity.
* **Franklin Templeton's role** reflects traditional finance's growing interest in secure, AI-compatible transaction frameworks.

### The Agentic Economy
This move comes as interest grows in autonomous agent frameworks that can handle payments or treasury tasks without direct human input. Trust and accountability infrastructure will be crucial if such systems are to manage real capital safely and at scale.
