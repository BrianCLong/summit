# Persona & Campaign Alert Workflows

## Commander's Intent
This module introduces human-in-the-loop analyst workflows for reviewing and triaging the `RiskAlert`s generated by the Risk-Based Alerting Engine. The goal is to ensure high-severity risks are rapidly surfaced to human analysts with context (links to workbenches) and a clear state machine, enabling swift defensive responsiveness without relying on unverified auto-remediation.

## Alert Review States
Alerts transition through three primary states during the triage process:
1. **`NEW`**: The alert has been generated by the engine and is waiting in the queue.
2. **`IN_REVIEW`**: An analyst has acknowledged the alert and is actively investigating the persona or campaign using the provided workbench links.
3. **`HANDLED`**: The analyst has completed the investigation. The entity may have been cleared, or action (e.g., adding to a watchlist, generating an external report) has been manually initiated. Once an alert is handled, it is no longer evaluated for escalation by the engine, though a new alert could trigger if thresholds are met again by *new* events.

## Workflow Execution
Analysts interact with alerts primarily through the alerts CLI (`summit/cli/alerts.py`) or corresponding internal UI views:
- **List Active Alerts:** `python summit/cli/alerts.py list`
- **Filter Alerts:** `python summit/cli/alerts.py list --state NEW --type PERSONA`
- **Update Alert State:** `python summit/cli/alerts.py update <alert_id> --state IN_REVIEW --notes "Starting investigation"`

The output provides immediate context, showing the key reasons the alert fired and the internal path (e.g., `/workbench/persona/{id}`) needed to perform a deep-dive investigation.

## Abuse Analysis
**Potential Misuse:** Analysts could prematurely close (`HANDLED`) alerts without proper investigation, or alerts could pile up unaddressed, creating a false sense of security.
**Mitigation:** The workflow is strictly internal and state transitions are explicitly human-driven and audited via the `state_history` property on each alert. No automated blocking or banning occurs as part of this workflow. A complete audit log of who transitioned states and when is preserved, enforcing accountability.
