# Resilience Objectives (C.A.P.S. Framing)

Use this template to set personal or team objectives focused on resilience.

## C.A.P.S. Framework

### Confidence

- **Objective**: Build self-efficacy in handling complex system failures.
- **Signal**: Successful resolution of an incident with minimal supervision.

### Adaptability

- **Objective**: Successfully navigate a major pivot in project direction.
- **Signal**: Updated project plan and team alignment within 48 hours of pivot.

### Purposefulness

- **Objective**: Align daily tasks with the mission of the Summit platform.
- **Signal**: Able to articulate how current work contributes to GA readiness.

### Social Support

- **Objective**: Strengthen peer connection and support outlets.
- **Signal**: Weekly coffee chats or peer-led knowledge sharing sessions.
