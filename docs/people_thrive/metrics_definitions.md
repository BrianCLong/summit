# People Thrive Metrics Definitions

These metrics are used to measure the health of the Summit organization culture while preserving individual privacy.

## Core Metrics

### 1. Learning Objectives Count

- **Definition**: Total number of personal development objectives set in 1:1s across the org.
- **Goal**: > 1 objective per person per half.
- **Privacy**: Aggregated count only.

### 2. Resilience Objectives Count

- **Definition**: Subset of learning objectives specifically tagged with C.A.P.S. resilience categories.
- **Goal**: Demonstrates adoption of resilience training.

### 3. Incident Resolution Rate

- **Definition**: % of reported behavioral incidents resolved within SLA (10 business days).
- **Goal**: > 90%.

### 4. Support Network Touchpoints

- **Definition**: Number of unique events or meetings held by staff communities and support networks.
- **Goal**: Indicates active community engagement.

## Privacy Guardrails

- All metrics must be aggregated at the organizational or department level.
- No metrics can be exported with fewer than 5 data points per bucket to avoid de-anonymization.
- Free-text fields are strictly forbidden in metrics exports.
