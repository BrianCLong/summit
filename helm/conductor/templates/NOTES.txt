Conductor Rollout

* Provide database/redis credentials via an existing secret:
    --set secrets.existingSecret=my-conductor-secret
  or use the inline dev-only secret:
    --set secrets.databaseUrl=postgres://... --set secrets.redisUrl=redis://...

* Price-aware routing flags:
    --set priceAware.enabled=true
    --set priceAware.pricingRefreshEnabled=true
    --set priceAware.capacityFuturesEnabled=true
    --set priceAware.failOpen=false
    --set priceAware.forcePoolId=pool-a
    --set priceAware.reservedDiscount=0.8
    --set priceAware.pricingSignalsJson='{"pool":"hint"}'

* Extra environment:
    --set env[0].name=FEATURE_FLAG --set env[0].value=true
    --set envFrom[0].secretRef.name=my-shared-secret

* Migrations:
    --set migrations.enabled=true to render the hook job
    --set migrations.hook=true to run as pre-install/pre-upgrade
    --set migrations.seedPricing=true to load db/seeds/pool_pricing_v1.sql
