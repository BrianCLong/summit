# Retention & Preservation Guidance

## Preservation Chain

- Collect artifacts within the incident window.
- Hash artifacts and store in a tamper-evident chain.
- Record artifact identifiers in the incident packet manifest.

## Retention Policy

- Retain incident artifacts per contractual requirements.
- Maintain transparency log digests for audit validation.
- Enforce retention policy with policy-as-code export checks.

## Evidence Artifacts

- Preservation manifest (hash chain)
- Artifact hash list
- Retention window declaration
