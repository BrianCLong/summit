# Open Architecture Self-Score

Checklist for assessing compliance with DARPA open architecture and MODA criteria.

- Interface versioning and compatibility rules documented.
- Proprietary element treatment with evaluator stubs and disclosure boundaries.
- Technology-insertion plans and conformance tests published.
- Transparency log and witness-chain coverage for evaluator artifacts.
- Peer-review enablement and rights captured in evaluator bundles and capsules.
