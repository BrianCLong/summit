# DFARS 252.204-7012 Mapping

Mapping of clause expectations to system features and evidence.

- **Incident reporting:** CIR incident packets with reporting checklist, access-certificate metadata, replay token, transparency proof.
- **Preservation:** witness chain hashes for collected artifacts; retention per policy.
- **CUI safeguarding:** scope tokens with CUI handling flags; Coalition Egress Guard enforcement.
- **Auditability:** transparency log entries for packets and releasability manifests.
