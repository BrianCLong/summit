# OCT Advantages

## Strategic Advantages

- **Decomposable risk portfolio** replaces monolithic risk scores.
- **Outcome-calibrated weights** tie signals to observed incidents.
- **Backtest artifacts** provide reproducible evidence for decisioning.

## Operational Advantages

- Learns **source trust weights** from outcomes and freshness.
- Supports **counterfactual analysis** for remediation planning.
- Produces replayable capsules with explicit policy bindings.
