# PCOV Advantages

## Strategic Advantages

- **Policy-compiled views** eliminate filter-after-fetch risks.
- **Stable ABI** enables long-lived integrations during schema evolution.
- **Auditable artifacts** provide deterministic evidence of access scope.

## Operational Advantages

- Pushdown redaction into compiled views for performance and safety.
- Cache views by policy scope to reduce latency.
- Produce witness records per request for compliance.
