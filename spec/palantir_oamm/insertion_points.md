# OAMM Technology Insertion Points

Open insertion points are designed for evaluator and partner contributions.

## Plug-in ABIs

- Analytics plug-ins for alternate scoring or analytics stacks.
- Transform plug-ins for data normalization or enrichment.
- Policy check plug-ins for access, disclosure, or compliance enforcement.

## Replacement planning

- Counterfactual insertion plans simulate module swaps and report performance deltas.
- Conformance tests validate plug-ins against interface contracts before activation.
