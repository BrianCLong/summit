# FOPB-LG Advantages

- Optimizes cost and latency by routing generation workloads between edge and cloud while preserving policy compliance.
- Enforces tenant-aware safety and privacy constraints, reducing risk of cross-tenant leakage during federated operations.
- Provides unified provenance across fragmented requests, enabling trustable audit trails for generated content.
- Supports dynamic carbon-aware routing to minimize environmental impact without degrading service levels.
