# OAEAC Advantages

- Unifies detections across operational, security, and behavioral telemetry to reduce alert fatigue and missed correlations.
- Delivers policy-aware responses with auditable provenance, improving regulator confidence and post-incident reporting.
- Learns from prior remediations to shorten mean time to respond while limiting customer-facing impact.
- Provides human-reviewable explainability vectors so analysts can override or reinforce automated actions.
