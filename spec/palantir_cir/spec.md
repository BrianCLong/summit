# Palantir CIR: Cyber Incident Reporting Automator

Automates DFARS-aligned incident packet creation with forensic preservation and reporting checklist.

## Capabilities

- Detects incident triggers from policy definitions; initiates preservation actions.
- Builds incident packet with timeline, impacted assets, CUI scope, forensic commitments, reporting checklist.
- Generates replay token and transparency log digest; supports counterfactual classifications.
