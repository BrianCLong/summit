# CNLS Advantages

## Strategic Advantages

- **Lineage-first analysis**: detects origin, resharing, and remix chains beyond
  network visualization.
- **Stance drift detection**: identifies reframing and sentiment inversion over
  time with explainable paths.
- **Replayable capsules**: deterministic evidence packages with commitments and
  provenance for audit-ready workflows.

## Operational Advantages

- **Cross-platform linkage** via normalized fingerprints rather than internal
  platform IDs.
- **Policy-aligned disclosure** through budgets and witness chains.
- **Coordination signals** derived from synchronized behavior and shared lineage.
