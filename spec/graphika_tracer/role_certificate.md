# Role Certificate

Role certificates provide auditable evidence for actor-role assignments.

## Fields

- Actor identifier and assigned role.
- Role score and confidence interval.
- Support subgraph reference (edge/node IDs).
- Merkle root commitment over support identifiers.
- Replay token and optional TEE attestation reference.

## Usage

- Gate downstream automation (disruption, monitoring, escalation).
- Provide reproducible evidence in governance reviews.
