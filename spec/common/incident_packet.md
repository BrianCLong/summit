# Incident Packet

Canonical incident reporting bundle aligned to DFARS 252.204-7012 expectations.

- **Triggers:** incident policy definitions mapping SIEM patterns to DFARS cyber incident criteria.
- **Contents:** incident timeline, impacted assets, CUI scope determination, preservation actions, reporting checklist.
- **Forensic commitment:** witness chain hash linking collected artifacts; optional attestation quote.
- **Replay token:** binds to SIEM index versions, time window, policy revision.
- **Transparency:** digest logged to append-only ledger; export includes contract clause mapping section.
