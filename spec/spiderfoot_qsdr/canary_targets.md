# Canary Targets (QSDR)

## Canary Types

- Decoy domains and URLs.
- Decoy accounts and email addresses.
- Instrumented endpoints to detect active probing.

## Generation Rules

- Must be isolated from real entities.
- Instrumented to detect outbound contact attempts.
- Bound to policy-defined trigger conditions.

## Outputs

- Canary catalog with commitments to target metadata.
- Witness ledger entry for canary set generation.
