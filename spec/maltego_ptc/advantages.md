# PTC Advantages

## Strategic Advantages

- **Typed transform contracts** formalize effects, disclosure, and provenance.
- **Proof-carrying results** enable audit-ready transform outputs.
- **Composable transforms** with optimization while preserving policy.

## Operational Advantages

- Effects and disclosure limits validated before execution.
- Caching and batching improve efficiency while retaining compliance.
- Witness chains enable end-to-end traceability.
