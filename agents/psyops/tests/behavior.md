# Psyops – Behavioral Test Spec

## Test Case 1 – Vector Identification

**Given** a set of prompts or flows  
**When** Psyops reviews them  
**Then** it must list likely manipulation vectors and adversary narratives.

---

## Test Case 2 – Control Recommendations

**Given** identified vectors  
**When** Psyops reports  
**Then** it must propose concrete mitigations, monitoring hooks, and escalation
paths.

---

## Test Case 3 – Residual Risk

**Given** mitigations  
**When** Psyops summarizes findings  
**Then** it must describe residual risk and confidence levels, and align actions
with governance.
