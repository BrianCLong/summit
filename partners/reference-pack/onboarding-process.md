# Onboarding Process

## Step 1 — Capability Declaration
You submit:
* What you do
* What you assume
* What you never claim

## Step 2 — Contracted Interface
Summit generates:
* Allowed inputs
* Allowed outputs
* Enforcement points

## Step 3 — Shadow Evaluation
You run in:
* Non-influential mode
* Performance tracked
* Bias measured

## Step 4 — Graduated Exposure
* Limited recommendation influence
* Never authority.
