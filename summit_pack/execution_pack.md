## 3. Golden Demo Plan

**Narrative: The 5 Beats**
1.  **The Threat: Ingesting the Unknown:** Begin with raw, unstructured signals from a simulated disinformation campaign and a suspected sanctions evasion network. Show the initial chaos.
2.  **The Connection: Entity Resolution:** Watch the platform instantly resolve disparate data points into coherent profiles, highlighting hidden links.
3.  **The Visualization: The Graph:** Navigate the interconnected web of actors, organizations, and financial flows, visualizing the adversary's TTPs.
4.  **The Analysis: Alerting and Notes:** Demonstrate automated risk scoring and the analyst's ability to append insights directly to the graph nodes.
5.  **The Action: Disruption and Export:** Export the findings as a compliant, actionable intelligence package ready for law enforcement or policy intervention.

**Required Features**
*   High-throughput data ingestion pipeline visualization.
*   Real-time entity resolution engine with confidence scoring.
*   Interactive, high-performance graph visualization (IntelGraph UI).
*   Automated alerting based on predefined threat TTPs.
*   Analyst workspace for note-taking and collaborative case building.
*   Compliance-ready export functionality (redacted PDF/JSON).

**Dataset Requirements**
*   **Type:** 100% Synthetic and pre-approved data.
*   **Content:** Fictional entities, non-existent financial transactions, and simulated social media posts reflecting a disinformation operation and sanctions evasion.
*   **Constraint:** Absolutely no real PII, sensitive operational data, or classified intelligence. All data must pass the pre-summit compliance gate.
*   **Owner Role:** Data Steward

**Preflight Checklist**
*   [ ] Verify the synthetic dataset is loaded and indexed.
*   [ ] Confirm all PII redaction rules are active in the UI.
*   [ ] Test the 5-beat workflow end-to-end on the staging environment.
*   [ ] Ensure the audit logging system is capturing all demo actions.
*   [ ] Validate that no sensitive secrets or credentials are visible on screen or in browser consoles.
*   [ ] Check internet connectivity and presentation display settings.
*   **Owner Role:** Demo Sheriff

**Fallback Strategy**
*   A fully recorded, high-resolution video of the entire Golden Demo, narrated by the Senior Intelligence Analyst.
*   The video will be stored locally on the presentation machine and available on a backup USB drive.
*   If the live environment experiences latency > 5 seconds or any error, the Demo Sheriff will immediately instruct the presenter to switch to the video.
*   **Owner Role:** Producer

## 6. Compliance Gate Checklist

This checklist must be fully executed and signed off prior to any live demonstration.

*   [ ] **Data Provenance:** Verify all data used in the demo environment is 100% synthetic or explicitly approved for public demonstration. No production data is present. (Owner: Data Steward)
*   [ ] **PII Redaction:** Confirm that the UI's PII redaction rules (e.g., masking names, obscuring locations) are actively enforcing privacy boundaries. (Owner: Compliance Lead)
*   [ ] **Least-Privilege Access:** The presentation account has the absolute minimum permissions required to execute the Golden Demo. It cannot access underlying infrastructure or sensitive configurations. (Owner: Security Lead)
*   [ ] **Audit Logging:** Verify that comprehensive audit logging is enabled and capturing all user actions within the demo environment. (Owner: Engineering Lead)
*   [ ] **Secrets Hygiene:** Ensure no API keys, database credentials, or other secrets are visible in the UI, browser console, or presentation materials. (Owner: Security Lead)
*   [ ] **Recording Permissions:** If the session is being recorded, confirm that all necessary consent forms and notices are in place according to relevant regulations (e.g., GDPR). (Owner: Legal Counsel)
*   [ ] **Sign-off:** The Compliance Lead has reviewed the environment and explicitly authorized the demonstration to proceed.
