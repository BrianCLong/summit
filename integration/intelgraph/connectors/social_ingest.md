# Social Ingest Connector

## Purpose

Ingest social content streams into IntelGraph with provenance metadata suitable for ANFIS.

## Requirements

- Capture content, actor identifiers, timestamps, and reference URLs.
- Emit evidence bundles with redacted bytes and stable pointers.
- Attach policy decision tokens for ingestion and downstream access.

## Outputs

- Evidence bundles for content items.
- Witness chain entries for ingestion steps.
