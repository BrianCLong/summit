# Proof Service

## Role

Unified service to validate and store proof objects, certificates, and receipts generated by POAR, JPC, MRSGS, CEVH, and EAMS.

## Responsibilities

- Verify signatures, determinism tokens, and attestation quotes.
- Publish digests to the transparency log and return inclusion proofs.
- Provide verifier APIs to check obligations without re-executing workflows.
- Maintain policy-versioned allowlists for acceptable enclaves and signers.
