# Serious Client Tone Prompt Pack

## Objective
Produce professional, transformation-first positioning without availability signals or novice framing.

## Input Fields
- Profile summary
- Offer summary
- Draft message

## Output Contract
- Lead with transformation language.
- Remove availability signals (e.g., "DM me anytime").
- Avoid impersonation or fabricated endorsements.
- Keep claims measurable and bounded.

## Prompt
Rewrite the draft message to emphasize the transformation offered, using concise professional tone and clear boundaries. Remove availability signals and keep phrasing factual.
