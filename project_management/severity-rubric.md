# Severity Rubric

This document defines the severity rubric for assessing the impact of abuse incidents.

| Severity     | User Harm                                                           | Financial Loss                                | Legal Exposure                                                            | Reputational Risk                                                     |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Critical** | Widespread, severe user harm; PII or sensitive data breach.         | Major financial loss; >$1M impact.            | Significant legal or regulatory action likely; class-action lawsuit risk. | Major negative press; significant brand damage; loss of key partners. |
| **High**     | Significant user harm affecting multiple users; account takeovers.  | Substantial financial loss; $100k-$1M impact. | Potential for legal action or regulatory inquiry.                         | Negative press coverage; noticeable brand damage.                     |
| **Medium**   | Moderate user harm to a limited number of users; spam, minor scams. | Moderate financial loss; $10k-$100k impact.   | Low risk of legal action; violation of terms of service.                  | Some negative social media attention; minor brand damage.             |
| **Low**      | Minor user inconvenience; low-level spam or scraping.               | Minimal financial loss; <$10k impact.         | No significant legal exposure.                                            | Minimal reputational risk.                                            |
