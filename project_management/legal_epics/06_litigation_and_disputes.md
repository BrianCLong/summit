## Epic 6 — Litigation & Disputes Playbooks (be ready before you’re served)

1. Create matter management system: holds, tasks, budgets, counsel, documents.
2. Implement legal hold automation with acknowledgments and audits.
3. Build eDiscovery playbook and collection toolkit (hashing, manifests).
4. Standardize escalation for demand letters and threatened claims.
5. Create arbitration/class-action defense posture for consumer/SMB (where applicable).
6. Maintain outside counsel bench and pre-negotiated rates.
7. Build a “facts first” incident timeline discipline for dispute readiness.
8. Track dispute metrics: volume, time-to-resolve, root causes, cost.
9. Create settlement authority matrix and approval workflow.
10. Run annual mock discovery drill and tighten weak spots.
11. Delete uncontrolled document retention that creates discoverable chaos.
