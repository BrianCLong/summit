# Abuse Taxonomy

This document defines the official categories of abuse that we track and mitigate.

## Categories

- **Account Takeover (ATO):** Unauthorized access to and control of a user's account.
- **Payment Fraud:** Use of stolen or fraudulent payment information to purchase goods or services.
- **Refund Abuse:** Illegitimate or excessive requests for refunds.
- **Scraping:** Automated extraction of data from our platform in violation of our terms of service.
- **Spam:** Unsolicited, irrelevant, or inappropriate content or communications.
- **Insider Misuse:** Intentional or unintentional abuse of authorized access by internal users.
- **Marketplace Fraud:** Deceptive or fraudulent activities within a marketplace environment, such as fake listings or scams.
