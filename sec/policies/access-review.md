# Access Review Policy
- Quarterly access reviews for all roles with sign-off by data owner and security.
- Step-up authentication (WebAuthn) required for export and administrative roles.
- Joiner/mover/leaver automation: disable accounts within 4 hours of termination notice.
- Reason-for-access must accompany privileged actions; log with correlated audit IDs.
- Violations trigger incident response per `ops/runbooks/incident-response.md`.
