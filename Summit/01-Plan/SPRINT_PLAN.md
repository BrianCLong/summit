# 10-business-day Summit Sprint Plan

## Day 1 — Control & clarity

**Outputs by end of day**
* Charter v1 locked
* Workstream leads named
* Milestones + critical path v1
* Decision log started (first 3 decisions recorded)

**Moves**
* 60-minute war room
* Task the 24h sprint (Build/Ops/Comms)

---

## Day 2 — Scope freeze + dependency capture

**Outputs**
* Requirements + scope IN/OUT frozen (M1 gate)
* Dependency list with owners + dates (vendors, approvals, data, stakeholders)
* Budget placeholder + spend approval rule

**Moves**
* 30-minute checkpoint: “Anything not owned is dead.”

---

## Day 3 — Risk and readiness architecture

**Outputs**
* Top 10 risk register (owner, trigger, mitigation, deadline)
* Readiness checklist (what must be true to ship)
* “Rules of Engagement” memo v1

**Moves**
* Confirm signing authority + contracting lane
* Confirm external spokesperson + comms approval lane

---

## Day 4 — Build/Prep Alpha (end-to-end skeleton works)

**Outputs**
* First end-to-end run (even ugly)
* Issues list prioritized (P0/P1/P2)
* Rollback/backup plan draft

---

## Day 5 — Ops/Partners locked

**Outputs**
* All external commitments confirmed in writing
* Vendor SLAs / statements of work / purchase orders initiated or signed
* Logistics plan (or deployment plan) v1

---

## Day 6 — Beta + internal rehearsal

**Outputs**
* Full rehearsal / dry run
* QA checklist executed
* Training notes or runbook v1

---

## Day 7 — Comms package ready

**Outputs**
* FAQ v1
* External messaging (approved)
* Internal announcement draft
* Support/triage script (if anything goes sideways)

---

## Day 8 — Final hardening

**Outputs**
* Critical path items closed
* Security/privacy check complete (data map + retention + access)
* Go/No-Go criteria written

---

## Day 9 — Go/No-Go review

**Outputs**
* Formal Go/No-Go decision recorded
* Remaining risk acceptance recorded
* Final checklist at 100%

---

## Day 10 — Execute + immediate postmortem capture

**Outputs**
* Execution complete
* Metrics captured
* Postmortem bullets captured within 2 hours (fresh truth)
