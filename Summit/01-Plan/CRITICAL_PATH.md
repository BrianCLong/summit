## The 10-Card Rule
1.  **Only 10 cards allowed total**.
2.  To add one, you must delete or merge one.
3.  **Blocked Rule**: Any card in "Blocked" must have:
    *   Blocker described in one sentence.
    *   One human owner to clear it.
    *   Decision deadline (date/time).
    *   If blocked > 24 hours, it escalates.
