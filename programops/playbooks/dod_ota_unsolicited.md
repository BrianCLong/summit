# DoD OTA/Unsolicited Playbook

## Strategy
*   **Focus**: Rapid prototyping and transition.
*   **Vehicle**: Other Transaction Authority (OTA) or Unsolicited Proposal.

## Steps
1.  Identify requirement/gap.
2.  Engage with end-user/lab.
3.  Propose solution via OTA consortium or direct unsolicited proposal.

## Tips
*   Emphasize "Dual-Use".
*   Show measurable outcomes.
