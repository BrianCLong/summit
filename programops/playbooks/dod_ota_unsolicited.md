# DoD OTA / Unsolicited Playbook

## Focus
Labs, Centers, and Other Transaction Authorities (OTAs).

## Strategy
- **Engagement**: Direct to technical points of contact (TPOCs).
- **Vehicle**: OTAs for rapid prototyping.
- **Unsolicited**: Must demonstrate unique capability.

## Submission Checklist
- [ ] Quad Chart
- [ ] White Paper
- [ ] Cost Rough Order of Magnitude (ROM)
