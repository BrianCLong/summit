# DARPA BAA/ISO Playbook

## Strategy
*   **Focus**: High-risk, high-reward research.
*   **Engagement**: Heilmeier Catechism.
*   **Vehicle**: Broad Agency Announcement (BAA) or Innovation Solutions Opening (ISO).

## Steps
1.  Identify Program Manager (PM).
2.  Submit Executive Summary / Abstract.
3.  Receive encouragement.
4.  Submit Full Proposal.

## Tips
*   Focus on "System-level transformation".
*   Avoid incremental improvements.
